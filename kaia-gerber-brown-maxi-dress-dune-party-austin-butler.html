    Part Two&quot; Party With Austin Butler

    Kaia Gerber took her minimalist style out to party last night in London. The model supported her beau, Austin Butler, at the world premiere of his much-awaited sci-fi film Dune: Part Two. Later, they joined his castmates at a star-studded after-party.

    21 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Kaia Gerber took her minimalist style out to party last night in London.

    The model supported her beau, Austin Butler, at the world premiere of his much-awaited sci-fi film Dune: Part Two. Later, they joined his castmates at a star-studded after-party.

    For the evening out, Gerber pulled out the chicest bodycon maxi dress in a deep brown hue with subtle golden sparkles all over. She styled the timeless, sleeveless piece with strappy golden heels, a matching golden leather clutch bag, and a black mini fur coat, which she carried on her arm. As always, Gerber kept her makeup look glowy and minimal, and she wore her brunette hair down in relaxed waves.

    Neil Mockford / Ricky Vigil M//Getty Images

    Holding his girlfriend’s hand as they walked to the fete, Butler looked cool as ever in an Elvis Presley–esque ensemble by Gucci. The Elvis actor layered a plunging jet-black double-breasted blazer over a white tank and paired it with matching loose black suit pants. He further channeled the King of Rock ’n’ Roll in a pair of black leather boots with a block heel, and a dainty silver chain necklace.

    Butler has been evoking Presley throughout his entire Dune: Part Two press tour—which has given us oh so many incredible fashion moments from the cast.

    Earlier this month, at an event for the film in Mexico City, Butler sported an all-black fit from Saint Laurent’s Spring/Summer 2024 ready-to-wear, consisting of a silky, plunging, glittery blouse layered under a long pinstripe blazer and tucked into matching high-waisted bell-bottom suit pants. The blazer had big shoulder pads, which jetted up and out to the sides, creating a dramatic silhouette, while Butler’s wide-leg pants were cinched at the waist with a black leather belt with a silver buckle. The actor finished the ensemble with the same heeled boots he wore last night—footwear Presley often wore during his Las Vegas residency days.
