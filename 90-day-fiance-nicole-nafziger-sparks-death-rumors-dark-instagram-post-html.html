    Nicole Nafziger has gotten in trouble for her misleading posts in the past. The former 90 Day Fiancé star has been known to post cryptic things on her Instagram, only for the link attached to the post to have nothing to do with what she posted. Though fans know this about her, one of her recent Instagram Stories still had followers concerned.

    What did ’90 Day Fiancé’ star Nicole post?

    Recently, Nicole posted a photo of herself in black and white, crying with the caption, “#RIP Devastated to confirm she died.” She also posted the photo to her Instagram Stories with the same caption. Though Nicole has posted #RIP posts that turned out to be clickbait before, the fact that she wrote that it was confirmed that someone died made the post more worrying.

    Nicole disabled the comments on her post so fans had to ask if she was OK in the comments’ section of previous pictures. However, when people swiped up on the post, it redirected to an article about celebrities who had died in 2021. The photo was later deleted.

    ’90 Day Fiancé’ fans were upset with Nicole

    Once fans noticed what Nicole was doing, they took to other photos to comment.

    “You turned the comments off your new post cause you know what people will say,” one fan commented on a photo.

    “I liked you until now,” another person wrote. “You’re not dead smh all for money”

    “I’m not sure what is real anymore since you keep posting fake death clickbait bullsh*t,” another user said.

    “Can you stop posting clickbait bullsh*t and then disabling comments,” one person added. “So annoying.”

    Nicole pretended to be pregnant

    Nicole doesn’t only fake deaths for clickbait, she has also teased being pregnant a number of times. Last year, she posted a picture of herself and a sonogram and added the words, “all because two people fell in love. #linkinbio #soexcited” to the post.

    She later cleared up the confusion.

    “No, I am not pregnant,” she said. “Sorry for the confusing post. Hope everyone is staying safe out there! Peace and love to all.”

    The post has since been deleted.

    What is Nicole doing after ’90 Day Fiancé’?

    Since her breakup with her 90 Day Fiancé beau, Azan Tefou, Nicole has decided to focus on herself. She recently announced that she was back in school.

    “Here’s a couple [of] life updates with me,” she captioned a photo on Instagram. “I made a big decision in my life. I’m enrolled in college at SCF! It has been a challenge going back to school after so long. But I am determined to make a better life for me and my daughter. I am currently working on pre recs to get into the Radiology program. I’m so blessed to have the supportive family that I do. They help keep me going on the days that I doubt myself.”

    She also used the post to confirm her breakup.

    “For those who have asked, my relationship with Azan has ended,” she continued. “I am currently concentrating on school and making sure my focus is on what’s important.”
