    John Molner – Wiki, Bio (Age)

    The husband of Katie Couric was born in Chicago, Illinois, on January 21, 1963, the son of Paula Molner and Herbert Molner. John grew up with his two brothers David Molner (a film financier) and Tom Molner (a lawyer).

As for his college education, he first studied at Trinity College and later graduated from the Chicago Booth School of Business. He was then hired as a financier by the New York-based private bank Brown Brothers Harriman.

    After investing his expertise for several years at Brown Brothers, John Molner retired in 2014. Despite his retirement, the Chicago-born financier continues to serve as a senior advisor to the bank.
