    Meaning of Hater Shit by Future

    09 Apr 2024, 00:00

    In the world of hip-hop, Future is a name that needs no introduction. With his unique blend of trap beats and melodic flow, the Atlanta rapper has carved out a niche for himself in the industry. One of his standout tracks, "Hater Shit", explores themes of success, resilience, and overcoming adversity. Through his lyrics, Future conveys a powerful message of rising above the negativity and embracing one's own greatness.The chorus of "Hater Shit" sets the tone for the entire song, as Future proclaims, "Little niggas get murked around with that hating shit, Little niggas see the dirt, fucking with that hating shit." Here, he addresses the toxic culture of envy and negativity that often surrounds success. Instead of succumbing to the hate, Future encourages listeners to focus on their own accomplishments and rise above the petty criticisms of others.One standout lyric that helps develop the theme of success is "El Chapo my idol, My watch as a chain, it's a brighter." Here, Future draws a parallel between himself and the infamous drug lord, El Chapo. While this may seem like a controversial statement, Future is not glorifying El Chapo's actions but rather his entrepreneurial spirit and ability to rise to the top. By equating his watch to a bright chain, Future is highlighting the tangible symbols of success and wealth that he has achieved.Another striking lyric in the song is "Full grown 36 zones, ain't no flex on it." This line refers to Future's drug-dealing past and the vast territories he once controlled. However, it can also be seen as a metaphor for his current dominance in the rap game. Just as he used to control 36 drug zones, he now exerts the same level of influence and power in the music industry. This lyric emphasizes the theme of resilience and transformation, as Future has overcome his past and turned his life around for the better.Additionally, Future touches on themes of love and loyalty in the line, "You take her on dates and I won't, You holding her hand and I won't." Here, he challenges societal norms and rejects traditional notions of romance. Rather than conforming to the expectations of others, Future asserts his independence and chooses to express his love in his own way. This lyric subtly reinforces the overarching theme of the song â€“ staying true to oneself and not allowing others to dictate one's path.Overall, "Hater Shit" is a powerful anthem that celebrates success, resilience, and individuality. Future's lyrics delve deep into the complexities of his own journey and speak to the experiences of many others in similar circumstances. The song promotes a message of empowerment and encourages listeners to rise above hate and negativity. Through his imaginative storytelling and creative wordplay, Future continues to solidify his status as one of hip-hop's most influential artists.
