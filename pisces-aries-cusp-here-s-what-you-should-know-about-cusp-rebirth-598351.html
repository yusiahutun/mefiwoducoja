    If you are born between March 17 to March 23, then you were born on the Pisces-Aries Cusp. Pisces is the last and Aries is the first zodiac sign in the astrological chart. So, you were born on the cusp of rebirth. You are a go-getter type of person who is intuitive, impulsive and imaginative. You have both the traits of these two zodiac signs. So, here’s what you should know about Pisces-Aries Cusp.

    Pisces-Aries Cusp:

    Strengths of Pisces-Aries Cusp

    You were born on the cusp of rebirth. So, you are empathetic, fun, quirky, smart, intuitive, creative. You make a great leader as you create new ideas, make great plans and smartly execute them. You are a great adviser also who carefully listens to other’s problems and gives thoughtful solutions to them. Being an empathetic person, you have great compassion for all your loved ones and you do care for them.

    Weakness of Pisces-Aries Cusp:

    You have a completely different vision to see this world from a new perspective. But this might seem to be odd to others. So, think a bit about your ideas before presenting to others. You stick to your beliefs and get highly stubborn for them. Just ease your stubbornness and listen to others for a while. You easily turn any conversation into a big debate. So relax and enjoy a quality conversation with others.

    Compatibility:

    You have an eccentric personality that can go well with all Air signs- Gemini, Libra, Aquarius. But Earth signs, Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn can also provide strong and grounding support to your life.

    Advice for Pisces-Aries Cusp:

    You like to give new ideas and advise but try to listen to others as well and let them speak. You are smart and intelligent but try to improve your listening skills a bit.
