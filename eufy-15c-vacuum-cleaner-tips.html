    9 Tips to Get the Most From Your Eufy 15C Robot Vacuum Cleaner

    A robot vacuum is a great way to help you save time while keeping your floors clean. The Eufy RoboVac 15C provides low-cost floor cleaning with a number of great features. We're here with some tips to help you get the most out of the robot vacuum.

    21 Apr 2024, 00:00

    A robot vacuum is a great way to help you save time while keeping your floors clean. The Eufy RoboVac 15C provides low-cost floor cleaning with a number of great features. We're here with some tips to help you get the most out of the robot vacuum.

    1. Set Scheduled Clean Routines

    Creating an automated scheduling routine is one of the best reasons to buy a robot vacuum. And with the 15C scheduling tool, you can tell the robot to clean the floor at a specific time of day.

    Make sure you've downloaded the Eufy Clean app for iOS or Android. Once the vacuum is connected, select the RoboVac device in the app and then tap Schedule at the bottom. From the scheduling dashboard, you'll see timers for each day of the week.

    Close

    2. Organize Your Power Cords

    Before starting a cleaning session, it's a good idea to pick up any power cords lying around the floor, as they can get stuck in the vacuum's brush or wheels.

    Thankfully, cable ties are included in the starter kit to organize power cords prior to cleaning. So make sure to spend a few minutes using them wherever you want to use the vacuum.

    3. Use the Quick Cleaning Configuration for Small Rooms

    The RoboVac 15C has four cleaning modes, but most users choose the default Auto Clean. While Auto Clean works well, it can take hours to complete. On the other hand, Quick Clean lasts just 30 minutes and should be sufficient for small rooms.

    To use it, select the Quick Clean button at the bottom of your 15C hardware remoteâ€”it's the button with a 30-minute timer symbol on it. From the Eufy Clean app, select the RoboVac device and then tap Mode at the bottom. You'll see the same 30-minute timer symbol below the on/start button.

    Close

    4. Master the Charging Stand

    The charging stand must be connected to a power outlet with the included AC adapter. Once plugged in, the charging stand is visible to the robot's sensors, meaning the robot can return to base when almost out of power.

    The RoboVac 15C can be instructed to return to its charging post at any time. Select the Home button on the hardware remote or the app. The 15C will halt its cleaning routine and lock into position on the charging stand.

    5. Enable BoostIQ for Increased Suction Power

    A hidden gem on the Eufy 15C is the BoostIQ configuration, accessible from the hardware remote when operating Auto or Quick Clean mode. BoostIQ is designed to increase your vacuum's suction power when needed, like when moving from hard floors to carpets.

    The feature can be switched on manually. Using your hardware remote, press the fan button. You should hear the device chime twice to confirm BoostIQ is active.

    If that isn't good enough, try pressing it again to choose the 15C's highest performance setting. The device chimes three times when in maximum power mode.

    6. Use Manual Control for a Better Clean

    While the 15C is designed to automatically travel around your home and clean, there may be times when you need to clean a certain area. You can do that with manual navigation.

    On the remote, and through the app, there is a directional pad that allows you to rotate the vacuum and brings the ability to move it forward or reverse.

    7. Empty the Dust Collector for Efficient Cleaning

    The vacuum's dust collector stores debris in a small tray situated on the side of the device. Once the tray is full, you can remove the dust collector by unscrewing it and pressing the release switch in the blue inset. Make sure to empty the collector often to keep the robot vacuum running efficiently.

    8. Control With Your Voice via Amazon Alexa or Google Home

    Thanks to support for Amazon Alexa and Google Home, you can control the 15C with a simple voice command. Some of the different tasks you can accomplish include starting a cleaning cycle and returning to the charging dock. You'll just need to set up access to Eufy from the Amazon Alexa or Google Home app.

    Once completed, if you're an Amazon Alexa user, you can create an Alexa Routine that combines multiple tasks. For example, you create a routine that starts the vacuum and turns on lights around the home.

    9. Give Spot Clean a Try

    From your hardware remote or mode selection in the companion app, press the swirl icon to launch Spot Clean. In Spot Clean, the RoboVac will circle the same spot repeatedly while operating at high power. It's a great way to clean up a problem area.

    Make Cleaning Easy With the Eufy 15C

    As you can see, the Eufy 15C is a perfect way to automate repetitive household tasks. Enjoy keeping your floors clean without having to lift a finger.
