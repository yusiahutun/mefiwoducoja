    Erin Andrews' Wedding: Get All the Details on the Star's Personal Life

    Sportscaster and host Erin Andrews has graced TV screens for nearly 20 years — but how much do you really know about the 39-year-old star’s career and personal life? Scroll down to get all the details on Erin!

    Erin hosting Dancing With the Stars in 2015. (Photo Credit: Getty Images)

    Is Erin Andrews married?

    Yes! Erin tied the knot with professional hockey player Jarret Stoll on June 24, 2017, at the Yellowstone Club in Big Sky, MT. The couple previously started dating in December 2012 and got engaged in December 2016 after four years together. “June 24th was so perfect! Can we do it again next weekend?” Erin captioned a wedding photo on her Instagram at the time — so cute!

    How tall is Erin Andrews?

    Erin is 5’10” tall! She always looks so tall when she interviews couples after their dances, doesn’t she?

    Does Erin Andrews have kids?

    No, but Erin and her hubby, Jarret, are parents to an adorable golden retriever puppy! The duo adopted their dog, Howard, a few months before their wedding and since then, Erin has loved posting cuter than cute pictures of “How How” on Instagram!

    Erin and her husband, Jarret.

    What is Erin Andrews’ net worth?

    As of 2017, Erin has a reported net worth of $58 million. You go, girl!

    Did Erin Andrews have cancer?

    While Erin is currently in remission, the Dancing With the Stars host previously battled cervical cancer. After being diagnosed with the disease in fall 2016, Erin underwent a successful surgery 17 days later on Tuesday, Oct. 11, 2016, to remove all traces of cancer. The reporter then told her oncologist she wanted to recover from her surgery in time for that Sunday’s pro football game. “I’m not watching any football games at home this year,” Erin recalled telling her doctor at the time. “This is [Fox NFL’s] Super Bowl year, and I’m not missing the Super Bowl.”

    Erin also gushed how supportive her then-fiancé, Jarret, was during that difficult time. “Let’s not worry about that right now. Let’s just get you better,” she recalled telling her to which she replied, “You wouldn’t miss a [hockey] game. You’d play through any injury, do whatever it takes to get back out there. That’s going to be me.”

    What was Erin Andrews’ lawsuit about?

    In 2008, a man named Michael David Barrett secretly filmed Erin through peepholes in two hotel rooms she was staying in while traveling for work. A year later, one of the videos, showing Erin walking around naked in her room, was released online and quickly went viral. The second tape was never made public. The star subsequently sued several entities — including Barrett, Marriott International, and Radisson Hotels — for negligence in relation to the incident and was seeking $75 million in damages. Erin was later awarded $55 million and Barrett served two and a half years in prison.
