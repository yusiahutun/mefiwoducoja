NEC Group Partners With AXS

06 Mar 2024, 00:00

BIRMINGHAM, UK (CelebrityAccess) — British venue operator NEC Group announced a new partnership with AXS that will bring mobile ticketing to Resorts World Arena and Utilita Arena Birmingham. Under the terms of the agreement, fans at the two venues will have access to AXS’ patented identity-based ticketing solution, AXS Mobile ID, with peer-to-peer transfer capability and integrated resale through AXS Official Resale offering. Additionally, the deal will see AXS assume the management of ticketing operations previously handled by the NEC Group’s internal ticketing arm, The Ticket Factory.
