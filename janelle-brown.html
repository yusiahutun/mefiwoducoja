    Rumors

    In 2010, during the show’s premiere, the Brown family became the subject of a police investigation. It was about the violation of Utah’s bigamy laws, which have strict rules about cohabitating with someone else.

    Then, the Brown family went to Nevada, where they could live openly and with more religious freedom. Later in 2012, the investigation was dismissed. But the family’s legal battles continued.

    The family pursued their lawsuit against Utah and stated that bigamy laws violate their constitutional rights, freedom of association, equal protection, free exercise of religion, and free speech.
