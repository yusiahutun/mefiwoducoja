    Top 10 TikTok Trends in August 2022

    06 Mar 2024, 00:00

    TikTok Trends in August 2022 have been incredibly creative! Here are the TikTok trends in August 2022! 

  • Taylor Swift’s “august”
  • I Could’ve Said That
  • It’s Corn
  • AI Death Prediction
  • “I’m Your Biggest Fan”
  • ABBA – Angeleyes (Dark Remix)
  • I’m Thirsty! Refreshing!
  • Insert Cash Or Select Payment Type 
  • Best of Both Worlds
  • Uncomfortable Laugh

    • 1. Taylor Swift’s “august”

    @sipenyukasenja_ #august#taylorswift ♬ august Taylor Swift – Kaylen

    Taylor Swift’s “august” on TikTok fits perfectly into the start of a new month.

    Set your favorite summer memories to this track and let it flow.

    2. I Could’ve Said That

    @_meluusOne thing bout me is, imma go even if I’m broke 😩🙃♬ Originalton – kida

    In this trend, using the audio clip from an interview with Ariana Grande, first share what you truly wanted to say in a situation, then share what you really said instead. 

    3. It’s Corn

    @ashleytisdaleITS CORN 🌽♬ It’s Corn – Tariq & The Gregory Brothers & Recess Therapy

    In an interview with a child, the child gets very excited about corn. Then this interview was turned into a song and started to spread on TikTok. Have a “corntastic” day! 

    4. AI Death Prediction

    @littlecrimester #ai #AI #mydeath #witch #fyp #sad #maryonacross ♬ mary on a cross – 𖤐

    Prediction of death with artificial intelligence, one of the most interesting and somewhat scary filters TikTok offers, attracted a lot of attention and became one of the trends of August. 

    On TikTok, users type “my death” into the AI greenscreen filter to see and then share an image with details and predictions about their deaths.

    5. “I’m Your Biggest Fan”

    @twentypercentdisgusting God tier #paparazzi #imyourbiggestfan #applejuice4lifebaby ♬ Lady Gaga Paparazzivmaperformance – ladygaga

    TikTok content creators show appreciation for the founders of their favorite things they love most with the lines of the iconic song Paparazzi by Lady Gaga. ‘’I’m your biggest fan. I’ll follow you until you love me.’’ 

    6. ABBA- Angeleyes (Dark Remix)

    @merlinragdollSomething changed when Merlin got his first tax bill♬ Devil Eyes – There I Ruined It

    Tiktok is a place where edits, creativity, and acting combine. In this content, you begin with the normal thoughts that will change dramatically afterward with the change of the music. It is one of the most impactful twists that TikTok has seen so far! 

    7. I’m Thirsty! Refreshing!

    @effzzdoggim thirsty 🌊💧refreshing😌♬ original sound – Nostalgia Hub

    This trend is one of the wildest trends, and it’s getting wilder. Since it is the summertime, if you need refreshing content, try this one out. Your clip will start with you saying I’m thirsty, then one of your friends will drop a full bucket of water on your face. And then you end your clip saying refreshing. (Clips shapes on how thirsty you are 🙂

    8. Insert Cash or Select Payment Type

    @bresbakedbeans im a big supporter of shamelessly asking bad dads for money at any time #daddyissuesatitsfinest#FreestyleFridays#daddyissues#insertcashorselectpaymenttype ♬ Glimpse of Cash – There I Ruined It

    People are taking this sound and mimicking how the self-scanner would look in real life. Simply lip-sync the sound and act as a self-scanner. Or, get creative and use the sound for situations that needs payment!

    9. Best of Both Worlds

    @arfiebaby lol #bestofbothworlds #childhoodtrauma #fyp ♬ original sound – tatamsounds

    Best of both worlds is a Hannah Montana song, and the creators use the line where she said, ‘’you get the best of both worlds’’ while they explain how two different things impact and shape your life.

    10. Uncomfortable Laugh

    @thesavvysagittarius But we are one step closer to an emergency fund #nospendchallenge #nospendaugust #crushyourmoneygoals ♬ emma uncomfortable laugh – cuppy💀

    We all experience awkward moments in our lives. And sometimes, when it is ‘too awkward, we choose to laugh to shake it off. This trend allows users to recreate those ‘too awkward’ moments.

    These were the TikTok Trends in August 2022! Do you have any favorite TikTok trends from August 2022 that we missed? Share with us down below or hit us up on our socials!
