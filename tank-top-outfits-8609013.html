    Tank tops are a tried and true staple—and for good reason. Although they’re easy to overlook in favor of more vivacious blouses, tank tops offer more variety, making them a closet staple worth the money. Even high fashion brands like Miu Miu and Loewe have taken to spotlighting the humble tank, decorating the timeless top with logos and pairing it with fanciful accessories.

    Whether they’re worn to provide extra warmth underneath outerwear or are donned on their own, don’t underestimate the power of tank tops as they can be the foundation for eye-catching outfits. The shirt’s sleek and simple silhouette allows it to be worn with almost anything, in case you want to keep it casual or you’re in the mood to dress to the nines.  

    There are countless ways to dress up a tank top, but we’ve narrowed it down to the best 11 ways to wear one this season.

    With a Corset

    Wear your tank top underneath a sultry corset for a luxuriously layered streetwear look. A monochromatic approach screams Dune, while a color contrast between your corset and tank oozes ‘80s vibes.

    Under Sheer Dress

    Get more wear out of your not-so-safe work items with the help of a tank top. A striped version paired with a sheer dress marries modesty with fun as the tank adds more coverage and color to the see-through garment. 

    With a Denim Midi Skirt

    There is no more classic duo than a tank top and a denim midi skirt. The staple summer set is tried and true for a reason as the easygoing items come together to create an effortless ensemble. 

    With Short Shorts

    Speaking of warm weather outfits, Daisy Duke-inspired short shorts and a tight-fitting tank are a match made in heaven. Opt for distressed denim and boots to complete the look. 

    Under a Power Jacket

    Tank tops are not just for casual occasions as a power jacket instantly elevates the wardrobe essential. Pair your favorite outerwear piece and tank with dark wash bootcut jeans for the ultimate boss babe outfit.

    With a Textured Skirt

    When you’re working with a sleek and simple top, wearing texture on the bottom will balance the outfit. Add some excitement to a basic tank with a textured skirt. Fringe, sequins, and lace are all welcome. 

    With Statement Pants

    Bold bottoms will breathe new life into a tank top, so don’t be shy and pull out a pair of brightly colored trousers to brighten up your outfit. 

    With Jorts

    Deliver the quintessential skater girl look with low-rise jorts and a dopamine-inducing hat for warmer days. Add a belt for more texture and bling.

    Under a Vest

    A tank top is the perfect addition to a vest, allowing the business casual item to take center stage in this utilitarian uniform.

    With Baggy Pants

    Little top, big pants reign supreme as wide-legged play nicely with itty-bitty tank tops. 

    With an A-Line Skirt

    A voluminous A-line skirt immediately creates an intriguing silhouette, while adding an air of elegance to otherwise informal tank tops.
