    Best powder bronzer

    Pat McGrath Labs Skin Fetish Divine Bronzer

    Best powder bronzer

    Pat McGrath Labs Skin Fetish Divine Bronzer

    Considering your undertones - warm, cool or neutral - is Pat McGrath's leading tip for finding a bronzer cohesive with your skin tone. The sheer lightweight colour of her brand's Skin Fetish Divine Bronzer is ultra buildable, allowing you to effortlessly create a healthy, controlled warmth and naturally sun-kissed glow as you apply. What's more, the range has eight wide-reaching shades to choose from.

    Best bronzer for shade range

    Fenty Beauty Sun Stalk'r Instant Warmth Bronzer

    Best bronzer for shade range

    Fenty Beauty Sun Stalk'r Instant Warmth Bronzer

    Before the Fenty Effect, most prestige brands only offered up bronzing products in a pitiful selection of shades. Now, spectrum-spanning line-ups are the standard, but Rihanna's gamechanging Sun Stalk'r bronzer remains the very best for cool, pale shades and rich, deep hues that don't lean too ashy or too red in tone.

    Best bronzing tint

    Laura Mercier Tinted Moisturiser Bronzer

    Best bronzing tint

    Laura Mercier Tinted Moisturiser Bronzer

    Fans of Laura Mercier's original Tinted Moisturiser should consider this tan-enhancing new sibling. This luminous bronze pearl texture melts into your skin for a healthy post-holiday warmth without ever looking streaky or overly orange. Either mix with your foundation or skin tint for an added boost of colour, or apply generously across your forehead, nose and cheeks for that subtle kiss of sunshine.

    Best stick bronzer

    Milk Makeup Matte Bronzer

    Best stick bronzer

    Milk Makeup Matte Bronzer

    A simple scribble and blend of this pigmented stick will award your skin with a realistic warmth; in short, a little bit goes a long way. The petite packaging is ideal for slotting into a small bag, or taking on short-haul trips, too.

    Best luxury bronzer

    Armani Beauty Luminous Silk Sunlit Creamy Bronzing Powder

    Best luxury bronzer

    Armani Beauty Luminous Silk Sunlit Creamy Bronzing Powder

    This luminous bronzer is summer skin in a compact. Think creamy, buildable application, flattering hues and a softlit glowy sheen that looks believable and smooth, without ever highlighting texture. Inspired by a post-holiday Mediterranean glow, the ultra-thin layers effortlessly for a custom bronze colour that wears seamlessly throughout the day.

    Best matte bronzer

    Nudestix Nudies All Over Face Color Matte

    Best matte bronzer

    Nudestix Nudies All Over Face Color Matte

    Perhaps the most-bookmarked beauty look of last year, Sofia Richie's 'quite luxury' glow inspired a host of bridal make-up looks. The star of the show? This creamy yet shimmer-free bronzer, which her make-up artist Pati Dubroff combined with the brand's dewier, punchier Picante blush. The two are a skin-energising match made in heaven.

    Best natural-looking bronzer

    Hermès Plein Air H Trio Healthy Glow Mineral Powder

    Best natural-looking bronzer

    Hermès Plein Air H Trio Healthy Glow Mineral Powder

    We've long been hooked on Hermès' lipsticks, but the brand's latest beauty launch has usurped the statement-making Rouge Feu bullet to become our most treasured piece in the collection.

    In short, the Plein Air H Trio powder is the ultimate complexion perfector, working to blur, brighten and bronze in one fell swoop. The mineral-based powder is so light it's almost impossible to over-apply, while the clever combination of colours imparts multidimensional warmth, bringing sun-starved skin to life. If you introduce one thing to your make-up kit this season, make it this.

    Best cream bronzer

    Chanel Les Beiges Bronzing Cream

    Best cream bronzer

    Chanel Les Beiges Bronzing Cream

    Product reformulations are always risky, so when Chanel tweaked the formula of its beloved Soleil Tan, loyal fans prepared to stockpile the original. Thankfully, the newer formulation – available in three shades – is even better than the first.

    The finish is similar, but a very well-trained eye will notice a slightly softer, glowier effect. The hydration-boosting kalanchoe extract is a welcome addition too.

    There's myriad ways to apply this radiant, mousse-like cream, but we like swirling it on beneath foundation for the most believable bronze. Those in the know swear by it for a grown-up contour, too.

    best bronzer for dry skin

    Charlotte Tilbury Beautiful Skin Bronzer

    best bronzer for dry skin

    Charlotte Tilbury Beautiful Skin Bronzer

    Charlotte Tilbury's anticipated bronzer really stands up to the hype that preceded it. Rich and creamy, but with staying power that impresses, the Beautiful Skin bronzer is ideal for anyone who wants hydration and satiny colour without reflective shine. The pan is large enough to swirl a fluffy brush evenly through, and the full-size mirror is helpful, too.

    Best mineral bronzer

    Dior Forever Natural Bronze

    Best mineral bronzer

    Dior Forever Natural Bronze

    Ideal for sensitive skin and blemish-prone types, Dior's bronzer is made using mineral pigments, so won't clog pores or prompt irritation. There are eight shades to choose from, and each imparts a soft-focus veil of warmth rather than obvious, high-pigment colour.

    Best bronzer with skincare benefits

    Drunk Elephant D-Bronzi Anti-Pollution Sunshine Drops

    Best bronzer with skincare benefits

    Drunk Elephant D-Bronzi Anti-Pollution Sunshine Drops

    Drunk Elephant’s smart offering is an antioxidant-packed liquid. Part skincare, part make-up, these clever drops can be mixed into foundation to deepen the tone and add radiance, or blended out over cheeks for a translucent, dewy result. Quite the cult hero (you can thank Tiktok), this one is very rarely in stock – so don't delay.

    Best illuminating bronzer

    Westman Atelier Liquid Super Loaded Illuminator

    Best illuminating bronzer

    Westman Atelier Liquid Super Loaded Illuminator

    Part highlighter, part bronzer, this sublime recent launch from Gucci Westman is designed, according to the make-up artist, to deliver that elusive 'golden hour on holiday' glow. Indeed, it makes skin look remarkably radiant, with nourising oils alongside glimmering pigment to hydrate and illuminate in one.

    Be sure to shake the bottle before application, and call on a fluffy stipple brush for the best blend.

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    Best liquid bronzer

    Glossier Cloud Paint

    Best liquid bronzer

    Glossier Cloud Paint

    Glossier's beloved Cloud Paint blush now comes in five bronzer shades. These tan-enhancing formulas work in much the same way as the fan-favourite cheek colour, delivering a sheer and shimmer-free wash of colour that doesn't slip and slide around on skin. Try using it to define your eyelids, too.

    Best bronzer for contouring

    Vieve Modern Bronzer

    Best bronzer for contouring

    Vieve Modern Bronzer

    Jamie Genevieve's clever bronzer is designed to deliver razor-sharp cheekbones. Each palette contains two tonal shades that can be combined to sculpt and shape the facial contours.

    The texture is the most buttery, cream-like powder we've tried, meaning you get both longevity and a glowing finish, with a discreet touch of shimmer. Your palette can be topped up with a low-waste refill, too.

    Best stick bronzer

    Rare Beauty Warm Wishes Effortless Bronzer

    Best stick bronzer

    Rare Beauty Warm Wishes Effortless Bronzer

    Anyone truly nervous about wielding a bronzing brush will find success with Rare Beauty's foolproof stick. This creamy, hydrating bronzer can simply be swiped over cheekbones in an upwards and outwards motion, then blended at the edges with a sponge or finger.

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    Best dewy cream bronzer

    Nars Bronzing Cream

    Best dewy cream bronzer

    Nars Bronzing Cream

    Inspired by the brand's legendary power bronzers, these creamy formulas offer the same beachy bronze with a more hydrating finish: ideal if you find traditional bronzing compacts tend to settle in your pores. The colour is well-pigmented but blends effortlessly, so there's no risk of ending up with an artificial-looking tan or giveaway streaks.

    Best shimmering bronzer

    Hourglass Ambient Lighting Bronzer

    Best shimmering bronzer

    Hourglass Ambient Lighting Bronzer

    A favourite with bridal makeup artists, Hourglass' Ambient Lighting bronzer has a marbled surface that means you can achieve a multi-dimensional finish with a single swirl. The lighter pigments add luminosity for a glow without the glitter. Try using it to set a low-coverage foundation.

    Best bronzer for beginners

    Benefit Hoola Matte Powder Bronzer

    Best bronzer for beginners

    Benefit Hoola Matte Powder Bronzer

    If you're new to bronzing, or simply a little nervous about application, consider Benefit's iconic Hoola powder. It's available in four shades, and looks incredibly natural, so you really can't go wrong. Apply it over cheekbones and the bridge of your nose in light, sweeping motions: the matte-yet-radiant powder illuminates while imparting translucent, blendable colour. Bronzing, for beginners.
