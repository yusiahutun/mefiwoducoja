    There are tons of tools and symbols that we can incorporate into our personal spells and rituals to make them more powerful and special—and if your goal is to boost your confidence or brighten your mood, then understanding the meaning of yellow candle magic is a great place to start. In general, candle magic is perfect for bringing the passion-fueled element of fire into any spell. And by combining that alchemy with the sunny, empowering, and focused vibe of the color yellow, you can craft spells that’ll infuse your intentions with the warmth and vitality of sunshine itself.

    If you want to incorporate some yellow candle magic in your own practice, it’s helpful to understand the symbolic meaning of the color yellow. In color psychology, the yellow is known to be cheerful, uplifting, and mentally stimulating. It can brighten your mood, wake up your brain, and promote positivity and joyfulness. Think of the energizing vibe of the sun rising or the cheerfulness of a bright yellow kitchen. These qualities are echoed in the spiritual meaning of the color yellow, too—as yellow in color magic is often associated with the warm and sparkly energy of the sun, as well as the confident and empowered solar plexus chakra.  

    You can wield the feel-good vibes of yellow in the world of candle magic, too. Lighting yellow candles—whether to set the mood or work some magic—can invoke feelings of joy and optimism, uplifting your spirit and filling your space with feel-good vibes. Yellow candle magic is especially useful if your intentions involve building confidence, tapping into your personal power, and connecting with more joy and optimism.

    • The Spiritual Meaning & Symbolism of Yellow Candles

      Yellow candle magic can be used to evoke the radiant, mood-boosting, and empowering energy of the sun. The sun is life-giving andfull of vitality—it’s literally the celestial body around which our planet orbits—so using sunny yellow candles for magical purposes is a great way to channel a little bit of that abundant and illuminating solar energy into your personal intentions. Astrologically, the color yellow is also associated with the clever planet Mercury, which symbolizes our thoughts and ideas. So yellow candles are great to burn if you’re manifesting new plans, brainstorming for a new project, or seeking mental enlightenment on something. 

      In some eastern religions and other spiritual practices, the color yellow is associated with a balanced solar plexus chakra—a term that refers to an energy center of the body relating to confidence, self-expression, and self-esteem. Yellow candle magic can be used to enhance these qualities, serving as a mystical tool for bolstering self-worth and inner strength. The energizing vibe of a yellow candle can also be used to awaken you to your own capabilities, encouraging you to step into your authenticity and embrace your one-of-a-kind star power with total conviction. Seeing the color yellow can help you connect with these things, but using yellow candle magic to intentionally work with these energies is even more effective.

    • Working With Yellow Candles in Spells & Rituals

      Whether you’re doing some full moon magic or casting a spell for a specific situation, yellow candles can be effective mystical tools for any ritual or intention that needs a boost of confidence, optimism, or warmth. Before conducting any ritual, be sure you’re doing so in a peaceful space where you can focus. You may also want to cleanse your ritual space or tools using incense, cleansing crystals like selenite, or simply with meditation before starting. And remember to always keep fire safety in mind when working with candle magic!

    • Sweet as Honey Attraction Spell

      What it Does:

      If there’s something going on for you that needs some sweetening up or a part of your life in which you want to build more confidence and pull in some good vibes, grab a yellow candle and get to working this attraction spell. This is the perfect ritual to perform if there’s a situation that feels bleak or negative, as it can help to bring warmth, positivity, and loads of abundance to the matter at hand.

      What You’ll Need:

      • Yellow candle
      • Sugar or honey
      • Dried chamomile or sunflower petals (optional)

      How to Cast:

      Place your yellow candle on a large plate, then sit in a comfortable place and focus on whatever situation you’d like to sweeten up and turn in a more positive direction. As you do so, light your yellow candle. Allow your eyes to focus on the light of the flame and meditate on your situation, visualizing various optimistic outcomes unfolding, no matter how unlikely they may seem. Imagine the people involved warming up to your desires or see yourself receiving good news. Now, imagine all of these thoughts being infused into the yellow candle itself, its flame transmuting your vision into reality.

      Next it’s time to seal your intention with a circle of attraction using either sugar or honey by creating an unbroken ring of the sweetener on the plate, completely encircling the candle. (If you choose to use dried chamomile or sunflowers in your spell, you can rub and crumble the dried flowers into your sugar before creating the circle, or sprinkle them on top of the circle of honey after it’s drawn.) By creating this circle of attraction around the candle, you’re infusing the situation at hand with an abundance of good vibes and optimism. Good luck!
