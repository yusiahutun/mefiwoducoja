    Much like Chelsea boots, combat boots are subtle and timeless enough to work with almost any outfit, from casual to formal, without pulling focus from the overall vibe. And while you can’t go wrong with a classic pair, designers regularly drop combat boots featuring trendy details like lug soles and shiny hardware so you can opt for a statement-making, rather than an understated, combat boots outfit. Whether you’re looking to add a bit of edge to your favorite ultra-femme ‘fit or want to level up your casual errand running looks, the 12 combat boot outfits ahead will keep your fashion inspiration cup brimming over, no matter the occasion. 

    A Midi Skirt and Oversized Sweater

    For a casual look that's both comfy and quietly luxurious, pull on your softest silk midi skirt, snuggliest oversized sweater, and a pair of stacked platform combat boots.

    A Mini Sweater Dress

    Supermodel and combat boot fan, Bella Hadid does a great job of wearing this style of boot in unexpected ways. Case in point: this snuggly sweater dress outfit. Rather than leaning into the hard edge inherent to combat boots, Hadid went fully in the opposite direction for a cozy and compelling look that's incredibly easy to emulate.

    A Jumpsuit

    Dr. Martens is a classic combat boot brand (this writer personally owns three pairs) and its surprisingly lightweight boots lend themselves to streetwear-inspired looks like a sleek jumpsuit and faux fur jacket.

    A Printed Midi Dress

    Thanks to their lace-up silhouette, combat boots are an exceptionally streamlined style of shoe which makes them uniquely suited for slinky or form-fitting dress styles like a sleek, halter midi-dress.

    A Graphic Mini Dress

    Instead of a pair of high-heels or booties, try wearing combat boots with your favorite graphic mini dress for a look that expertly blends hard and soft. Finish off this combat boots outfit with a brightly colored statement bag.

    A Luxe Blazer

    Lean into the luxuriousness of soft, leather combat boots by wearing them with a velvet blazer, leather mini skirt, and sleek fishnet tights.

    A Sheer Pinafore Dress

    Given how combat boots used to be synonymous with the punk and goth scenes, it's not surprising that this year's hottest and darkest trends like sheer dressing look great with this style of boot. A sheer, flowy dress that's equal parts witchy romance and high fashion plus a pair of understated combat boots makes for a dark and dreamy look.

    A Romantic Lace Blouse

    Calling all fans of dark academia; this one's for you. Add some edginess to a preppy tweed skirt and romantic lacy blouse by wearing them with sheer tights and a pair of chunky combat boots.

    A Slouchy Duster and Jeans

    While choosing outerwear can sometimes be a chore, a long coat can instantly elevate even the most basic of separates. Next time you want to feel dressed up without actually dressing up, pull on your favorite pair of jeans, snuggly beanie, duster coat, and slip on a pair of knee-high combat boots.

    Bike Shorts

    Make the most of oversized outerwear and a great pair of combat boots with this cute and casual combat boots outfit. A simple pair of black bike shorts is all you need to bring this trendy outfit together, but you can also go for an OOTD by adding colorful laces to your boots and carrying a purse in a matching color.

    Jeans and a Sweater

    While combat boots look great with skirts and dresses, that's not to say they can't also elevate a pair of jeans. For a casual combat boots outfit that's perfect for crisp fall days, try wearing a pair of shiny combat boots with light-wash straight-leg jeans and a warm, wooly sweater.
