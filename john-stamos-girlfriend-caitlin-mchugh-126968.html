    Now, this is the cutest thing we’ve ever seen!

    John Stamos took to Instagram on Sunday, March 5 to share an adorable photo of himself and his girlfriend, Caitlin McHugh, enjoying a trip to Walt Disney World in Orlando together — and his caption seriously melted our hearts!

    “I’ll be Prince Eric to your Ariel any ol’ time. @caitlinskybound #DisneyBound,” the Full House star captioned the sweet backside snapshot of the couple holding hands in front of the castle.

    Caitlin captioned a similar photo of the two, “Found my Prince ? #PrinceEric #KissTheGirl #DisneyBound #MagicKingdom #DisneyWorld #FairytaleLove (I know I look more like Vanessa, but I swear I have some natural red highlights).”

    John, 53, and Caitlin, 30 — who are 23 years apart! — began dating in early 2016 and have now been seeing each other since last March. The actor was previously married to actress Rebecca Romijn from 1998 until 2005.
