    Jacks jumping to Ellisville | Free News

    Officials at Alabama-based Jacks Family Restaurants a quick service restaurant headquartered in Birmingham announced that one of their Southern-brand eateries will open in Ellisville later this month.

    23 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Officials at Alabama-based Jack’s Family Restaurants — a quick service restaurant headquartered in Birmingham — announced that one of their Southern-brand eateries will open in Ellisville later this month.

    “We are proud to serve communities across the great state of Mississippi and are excited to bring our Southern-inspired, handmade menu, as well as our community partner spirit, to the people of Ellisville,” said Jack’s CEO Todd Bartmess. “With our fresh-menu items like our hand-breaded chicken and homemade Southern biscuits, locals are sure to enjoy our quality food prepared with tried-and-true Southern hospitality.”

    The opening Ellisville location, at 406 Hill St., marks Jack’s 30th Mississippi-based location. The building will feature the brand’s charming Southern architectural design, allowing for ample interior dining inside and a large window for guests to watch biscuits being made from scratch daily. There will also be a “drive-thru.”

    The first 50 customers at the location on opening day will receive free breakfast for one month. Additionally, the Jack’s app will house online exclusives, prizes and promotions for the following 12 weeks post opening.

    Individuals interested in employment opportunities are encouraged to apply for available part- and full-time positions. All Jack’s employees receive incredible benefits, such as health and dental insurance, flexible hours and weekly pay. Interested job-seekers can apply at eatatjacks.com.

    For more, visit eatatjacks.com. For offers and promotions, download the Jack’s app.
