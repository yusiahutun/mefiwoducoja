    All the 2024 Hair Trends We're Predicting, From Headbands to Choppy Bobs

    Headbands were spotted on the spring/summer 2024 runways (Helmut Lang, Molly Goddard, to name a couple), and recently on celebs including Jennifer Lawrence. If you're not ready to go full Blair Waldorf, keep the style sleek and simple like J.Law's.

    11 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Headbands

    Jose Perez/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

    Headbands were spotted on the spring/summer 2024 runways (Helmut Lang, Molly Goddard, to name a couple), and recently on celebs including Jennifer Lawrence. If you're not ready to go full Blair Waldorf, keep the style sleek and simple like J.Law's.

    Jennifer Behr Cruz Headband

    Jennifer Behr Cruz Headband

    Lawrence is a fan of Jennifer Behr's timeless headbands, like this minimalist leather style.

    Rich Copper Color

    If recent hair changes on celebs like Hari Nef and Meredith Duxbury are any indication, this shiny new shade of red will be the It color for 2024. Shvonne Perkins, master colorist at Madison Reed, agrees, calling "copper penny" one of her top color predictions for the year.

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    John Frieda Radiant Red Boosting Shampoo

    John Frieda Radiant Red Boosting Shampoo

    To keep color looking vibrant between appointments, swap in a red-enhancing shampoo like this one from John Frieda once a week.

    Choppy Bob

    The bob isn't going anywhere, but it is getting a bit more laid-back and unstyled. See: the recent cuts debuted by Laura Harrier, Emma Chamberlain, and Lady Gaga.

    Roz Santa Lucia Styling Oil

    Roz Santa Lucia Styling Oil

    Credit: Colorcard Studios

    After you blow-dry or air-dry your bob, run a bit of hair oil through to add shine. We love this non-greasy formula from Roz.

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    Honey Blonde

    Steve Granitz//Getty Images

    Another trending color as we head into the new year? This warm, medium shade of blonde recently spotted on the likes of Kim Kardashian, Rihanna, and Adele.

    ORIBE Bright Blonde Shampoo for Beautiful Color

    ORIBE Bright Blonde Shampoo for Beautiful Color

    Use a blonde-boosting purple shampoo, like Oribe's luxe formula, to keep color looking fresh, not brassy.

    Nouveau Bows

    Expect the ballet- and coquette-inspired bow trend to stick around, though it may look a bit different. For instance, we'll see corset-like ribbons (as seen backstage at Christian Siriano spring 2024), and supersized bows, like the one in Sarah Jessica Parker's hairstyle here.

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    Sandy Liang Palermo Bow in Black

    Sandy Liang Palermo Bow in Black

    Sandy Liang can be counted on for all things girly, including the perfect XL hair ribbon. Of course, a long ribbon from the craft store will accomplish a similar effect.

    Big Buns

    Seen at the 3.1 Phillip Lim and Alice + Olivia, the bun updo is a forever classic, and it's getting new life. This style Justine Marjan created for Olivia Culpo goes above and beyond by combining three major trends—bows, buns, and braids—in one.

    COLOR WOW Extra Mist-ical Shine Spray

    COLOR WOW Extra Mist-ical Shine Spray

    One thing that separates a messy bun from an elevated updo? Megawatt shine. Complete your look with some of this shine spray to get that reflective, professional finish.

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    Side Bangs

    Taylor Hill//Getty Images

    The rumors are true: Y2K-era side bangs are back, and Keke Palmer, Dua Lipa, and even Taylor Swift are giving the look their seal of approval. To keep the look firmly in 2024, avoid the flatironed-in-the-front, fried-in-the-back look of the past and go for a more cohesive style.

    Dyson Airstrait™ Straightener

    Dyson Airstrait™ Straightener

    Luckily, straighteners have come a long way in the last 20 years. Try the new Dyson Airstrait to not only save time in your morning routine, but also avoid added heat damage.
