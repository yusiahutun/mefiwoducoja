    Just when we thought no couple could surprise us quite like Kylie Jenner and Timothée Chalamet did, Rosalía and Jeremy Allen White emerged.

    The two stars were first spotted together late in 2023, a few months after they’d each split from their previous partners: Rosalía ended her engagement to singer Rauw Alejandro in July; and Allen’s estranged wife, Addison Timlin, filed for divorce in May.

    While the Spanish singer and the American actor have not yet publicly addressed their romance, they have been seen out together several times, looking cool in coordinating street-style looks—and usually accessorizing with a cigarette.

    Ahead, the couple’s full relationship timeline.

    January 19, 2024: They share another passionate kiss.

    White and Rosalía went on another—yup, you guessed it—PDA-filled outing, this time a hike in Los Angeles.

    In photos published by the Daily Mail, the Bear star is seen affectionately holding the Spanish singer’s face as they indulge in a steamy kiss. In other snaps, they’re seen gazing lovingly at one another and sharing a tight embrace.

    This time, however, the usually synced couple did not coordinate. Rosalía went bold, with a vibrant red hoodie paired with black leather pants and a capacious matching bag, while White opted for a light gray crewneck, black jeans, and a black beanie.

    January 11, 2024: They share a kiss.

    The couple is photographed making out against a car in Los Angeles in matching black outfits. Rosalía wears a bomber jacket, a miniskirt, and chunky over-the-knee boots, while White wears a black leather jacket, black pants, and a black beanie.

    January 3, 2024: They match in all-black looks.

    Rosalía and White coordinate in all-black looks for an outing in New York City. She wears a black cropped jacket over a baby-pink button-up, a black miniskirt with a high slit, sheer black tights, and pointy black heels. He goes casual in a black collared sweater with a matching leather jacket, loose trousers, and a beanie. He finishes the look with brown shoes.

    December 13, 2023: They engage in some PDA.

    The pair perform off some sweet public displays of affection during a smoke break in Los Angeles. This time, the “Bizcochito” singer wears a green leather bomber jacket with a pleated black miniskirt and lemon-yellow sandals, while White is cool in a blue jacket layered over a black vest and white T-shirt, paired with blue jeans, a braided black belt, and black boots.

    December 1, 2023: They hold hands in L.A.

    White and Rosalía kick off the month with a bang. The two are spotted holding hands in matching black looks in Los Angeles. She is chic in an oversize zip-up sweater, long skirt, pointy heels, a leather handbag, and a stretchy headband. He wears a black fuzzy sweater, black trousers, white sneakers, and a beige baseball cap.

    TheImageDirect.com

    November 29, 2023: They match outfits during a smoke break.

    In photos published by TMZ, the Motomami singer and the star of The Bear are seen in matching black and white athleisure looks while on a smoke break in West Hollywood. Rosalía wears a black zip-up sweatshirt with matching leggings and a headband, plus white sneakers with white long socks. White wears a plain white tee with black sweatpants, white sneakers with white socks, and a black beanie. The stars intertwine their legs as they chat and smoke.

    “Rosalía and Jeremy Allen White have recently started dating,” a source tells Us Weekly. “They started out as just friends, but things have turned romantic recently.”

    October 29, 2023: They’re seen together for the first time.

    Rumors that White and Rosalía are dating kick off when gossip account Deuxmoi publishes a tip claiming the pair attended a screening of the 1998 cult-classic thriller Wild Things together in Los Angeles.

    A few days later, on Sunday, October 29, the two are seen shopping for flowers at a farmers’ market together. Rosalía wears a blue hoodie with a white illustrated print over a white top and white shorts, paired with long white socks and chunky black boots with several buckles. White wears a fuzzy green cardigan over a white tank, plus black slacks, white sneakers, and a beige baseball hat.
