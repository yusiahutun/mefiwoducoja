    1. Windsor Golf Hotel and Country Club

    Windsor Golf Hotel and Country Club is amongst the most glamorous wedding venues in Nairobi. This amazing hotel is located at Kigwa Road Ridgeways. So, if you were searching for wedding locations in Nairobi with humungous capacity, Windsor Golf Hotel and Country Club is your spot.

    The hotel and country club has various wedding venues and amenities that will amaze you.

    • The Graden Pond that is perfect for memorable “I do” moments.
    • The Kigwa View, a human-made lake with a great ambience.
    • The 10th Tee that overlooks the expansive golf course and is one of the most favoured wedding photo session venues in Nairobi.

    The wedding venue charges differ with the different packages offered. Its Lake-view side can host 300 guests at approximately Ksh.100,000. The Garden Pond view can host up to 150 guests at an average cost of Ksh.75,000 while Kigwa Road View can host 500 guests and its approximately Ksh.120,000. Another popular location is the 10th Tee that can cost you about Ksh.145,000 for hosting 1,500 people.
