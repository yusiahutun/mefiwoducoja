    Anna Elisabet Eberstein is a TV producer from Sweden

    Born and raised in Sweden, Anna Eberstein now lives with Grant in London, per People. The same source states that she has a master’s degree in economics from Sweden’s Uppsala University and studied at Lincoln Memorial University in Tennessee. Eberstein reportedly went on to work as a promotions producer for ESPN, before going freelance as a TV producer.

    She co-founded a sock brand

    In 2014, the entrepreneurial Swede also co-founded a children’s slipper sock brand called Ace & Me with her cousin, according to People, now better known as Ace Slippers.

    Retailing at about US$16 per pair, the eco friendly brand describes its designs as “traditional Swedish moccasin slipper-socks” with “an edgier design” that are handmade and home-grown.

    She’s not into his films at all

    Eberstein doesn’t seem to be into romcoms as much as her husband is, according to one of Grant’s previous interviews.

    “My wife is the man in our house, really. She likes violent films, gangster films while I’m sitting there watching Roman Holiday or The Sound of Music or something,” he said per ET. “So she’s never liked those films I did in the past, which are all romance and stuff. But now she’s starting to get a taste for [my work].”
