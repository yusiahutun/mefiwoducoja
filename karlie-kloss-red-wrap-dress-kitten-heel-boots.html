    Karlie Kloss Brings the Glamour in Red Wrap Dress and Leather Boots

    Karlie Kloss brought the glamour attending a fashion fete in New York City last night. The supermodel was among the guests at W magazines VIP dinner, in celebration of its annual Originals issue, and she looked incredible in a bright red gown.

    23 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Karlie Kloss brought the glamour attending a fashion fete in New York City last night.

    The supermodel was among the guests at W magazine’s VIP dinner, in celebration of its annual Originals issue, and she looked incredible in a bright red gown.

    The dress featured a skintight top with a mock neck and long sleeves, as well as a breezy skirt. The piece was cinched at the waist with a silky built-in belt that wrapped around the Project Runway host’s torso in a zigzag pattern. Kloss styled the monochrome gown simply, with pointy black leather boots with chic kitten heels, gold hoop earrings, and layered rings. She wore glowy makeup, a wavy blowout, and a short white manicure.

    Raymond Hall//Getty Images

    Kloss has been lying low for the past few months since welcoming her second child with husband Joshua Kushner in July. The couple, who married in an intimate ceremony in New York in October 2018, had their first child, son Levi, in March 2021.

    Kloss revealed her second pregnancy at this year’s Met Gala, held May 1. The model walked up the carpeted steps of the Metropolitan Museum of Art in a black floor-length gown by Loewe featuring long sleeves and a yellow print that mimicked the shadow of her white pearl necklaces and belt from Garrard—the latter of which hugged her baby bump. She also wore black Jimmy Choo heels with bows on the front, white pearl-and-diamond earrings, and a diamond cluster ring.
