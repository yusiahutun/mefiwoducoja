    This is the moment a 'drunken' Celtic fan boots a busker's speaker while a man and woman are belting out a Rangers 'anthem' in Glasgow city centre. 

    The 45-second clip, uploaded to Twitter, shows a street performer playing Tina Turner's Simply The Best on the guitar, while two merry passersby provide the vocals on his microphone. 

    While the man and woman embrace each other and happily sing along, a Celtic fan appears less than impressed with the tune, which is often chanted from the stands by his rival's supporters. 

    The busker quickly takes his phone off the top of a speaker as the thug approaches and kicks it with force, stopping the music from playing.

    The female karaoke-singer then shouts: 'What the f*** man?' 

    Cheers can be heard from passing by Celtic fans, before the upset woman throws a plastic cup at the man's head.

    He then shouts: 'You f*****g bint'.
