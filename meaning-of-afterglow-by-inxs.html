    Meaning of Afterglow by INXS

    17 May 2024, 00:00

    The INXS song "Afterglow" is a haunting and introspective track that delves deep into the complexities of love, loss, and the lingering memories that remain. The song's poignant lyrics and melancholic melody work in harmony to create an emotional journey that resonates with listeners on a profound level.At its core, the overall theme of "Afterglow" can be interpreted as the aftermath of a relationship, the bittersweet residue left behind once a love has faded. Throughout the song, the protagonist finds themselves lost in the memories and emotions that surround their former partner. This theme of longing and yearning is beautifully captured in the lyrics.One standout lyric that serves as the foundation for the song's theme is "Bathed in blue, the walls of my memory divides the thorns from the roses." Here, the color blue symbolizes the melancholy that the protagonist feels when reflecting on the past. The walls of their memory act as a dividing force, separating the painful memories (thorns) from the beautiful moments of their relationship (roses). This lyric highlights the complexities of love and the melancholic beauty that can be found even in moments of sorrow.Another impactful lyric is "Touch me and I will follow in your afterglow." This line suggests that the protagonist is still deeply connected to their former lover, and their touch has the power to transport them back to the euphoria of their shared past. The idea of an "afterglow" represents the lingering essence of a love that has since faded, but still holds a strong influence on the protagonist. It speaks to the profound impact that a love can have on a person long after it has ended.Additionally, the lyric "I'm caught in your shadow, I'm losing control, My mind drifts away, we only have today" amplifies the sense of longing and the struggle to let go. The concept of being caught in someone's shadow implies that the protagonist is constantly reminded of their former partner, unable to fully move on. The contrast between "losing control" and the notion of living in the present moment further emphasizes the emotional conflict within the protagonist. It suggests that, despite their best efforts, they are unable to fully detach themselves from the memories of their past love.As the song progresses, the lyrics take on a more introspective tone. The line "I'm living in your afterglow" reflects a resigned acceptance of the protagonist's current state. They have come to terms with the fact that their former lover's presence still permeates their life. The afterglow becomes their new reality, a constant reminder of what once was.Overall, "Afterglow" is a compelling exploration of the lasting impact of love and the complex emotions that accompany its aftermath. Through its evocative lyrics, the song encapsulates the bittersweet beauty found within the remnants of a relationship. It serves as a poignant reminder that even in the wake of heartbreak, memories and emotions have a way of staying with us, shaping our journeys long after the love has faded.
