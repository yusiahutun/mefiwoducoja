    17 Apr 2024, 00:00

    In today's world, it's easy to make most of your wardrobe work year-round. Sleeveless dresses can be styled over turtlenecks in the winter while a cozy sweatshirt can be worn with biker shorts. Footwear also tends to be seasonless and no matter what the weather is doing outside, there's no shortage of ideas for how to style combat boots, AKA one of fashion's most versatile shoe styles.

    Combat boots continue to be a smart choice when putting together an outfit since they're easy to walk in yet still pack a punch. These shoes can toughen up your sweetest skirt, give your everyday jeans a boost, and even work with sweatpants. Check out our favorite ways to wear combat boots, ahead, then shop a cute pair to add to your closet.

    With Sequins

    Opposites do attract, and in this case, combat boots can be used to balance out statement-making sequins, making these sparkly pieces feel cool and a bit more casual.

    With a Skirt

    If you're dreading slipping on heels and aren't into ballet flats, go the grunge route with a pair of combat boots, which will instantly toughen up frilly, feminine clothing.

    With Jeans

    Sneakers might feel like the obvious choice when pulling on a pair of jeans, but combat boots will add extra edge and keep things interesting, even if the rest of your outfit consists of basics. If you really want to wow, play around with proportion and choose a platform pair with lots of volume.

    With a Suit

    Dress down your structured suit with a T-shirt, then add black pair of black combat boots. The result is a sleek look that also includes an element of playfulness, meaning it'll work for both business-casual office environments and your after-hours plans.

    With a Black Dress

    We've always considered a short black dress to be a wardrobe staple, but if your look could use a little spice, these shoes will quickly give this classic piece a punk, gothcore twist and make it feel more modern.
