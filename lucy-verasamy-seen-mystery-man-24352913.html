    Lucy Verasamy seen with mystery man in cosy snap after revealing she has new boyfriend

    Good Morning Britain and ITV weather girl Lucy Verasamy has caused a stir on social media after the presenter shared a snap of her roaming around with a mystery man on what appears to be a romantic museum date.

    15 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Good Morning Britain and ITV weather girl Lucy Verasamy has caused a stir on social media after the presenter shared a snap of her roaming around with a mystery man on what appears to be a romantic museum date.

    The forecasting beauty, 40, who presents the weather on ITV's breakfast show Good Morning Britain, was spotted admiring some art with the anonymous bloke at the Van Gogh Live exhibit at Kensington Gardens in London.

    Taking to Instagram to share a series of snaps with her 150k followers, Lucy documented her arty museum trip and could be seen standing in front of the huge, immersive art pieces during her culture-filled day.

    The first and second snaps show Lucy taking in the artwork from the famous Dutch painter, Vincent Van Gogh, with the second being a panoramic video directed by Lucy to show the famous 1889 painting, Starry Night.
