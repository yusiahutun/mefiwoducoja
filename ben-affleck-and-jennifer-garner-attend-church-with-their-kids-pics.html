    They may have just finalized their divorce in October 2018, but Jennifer Garner and Ben Affleck are committed to being the best coparents out there. It seems like the 13 Going On 30 actress and the Gone Girl actor are doing a pretty good job considering they were all smiles as they spent their Sunday Funday on April 28 at church with their three kids.

    Jen, 47, and Ben, 46, — who are the parents to Violet Affleck, 13, Seraphina Rose Affleck, 10, and Samuel Garner Affleck, 7 — shocked their fans in June 2015 when they announced they were getting a divorce after 10 years of marriage. Following the end of their romantic relationship this past fall, a source close to the former couple dished that the two are “continuing to work together to make their parenting as seamless as possible,” the insider revealed to People in November 2018. “They really like to do things as a family, be it outings, church, cooking or watching movies. They have both had some ups and downs this year, and they seem to be in a good place.”
