    Can they get any cuter?

    Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds looked amazing as they attended the 2017 Time 100 Gala on Tuesday, April 25 — proving that they truly are a power couple. The former Gossip Girl star was by her husband’s side as he was recognized as one of the 100 Most Influential People.

    The 40-year-old Deadpool star looked handsome in a navy blue suit, white button-down shirt, and black bow tie. But his wife stole the show — the 29-year-old looked absolutely stunning in a low-plunging fitted black dress with floral appliqué and fringe detail.

    MORE: Blake Lively on Googling Herself — “It’s Just Ended in Full Depression”

    Oscar Award-winning actress Helen Mirren wrote a tribute to Ryan for the Time 100 list and she gushed over the father-of-two — he shares two-year-old daughter James and six-month-old daughter Ines with Blake — and she praised him for his acting and for his support of women.

    “He likes them in the most simple, direct, unadorned way: for their humanity. (How very appropriate that he is the father of two girls),” the 71-year-old wrote.

    MORE: Ryan Reynolds Gushes Over Wife Blake Lively, Says She Keeps Him “Sane”

    It’s pretty clear that Ryan admires the women in his life — so much that he invited his wife and his mother Tammy along as his dates to the gala. He even posed for photos with his mom on the red carpet — and she looked lovely in an all-black ensemble.

    Ryan’s not the only person in his household to receive such an honor — back in 2011, Blake was also chosen as one of the 100 Most Influential. Ryan made this year’s list along with Ghostbusters star Leslie Jones, singer John Legend, and TV host James Corden — and he gushed over his achievement on Instagram.

    “Thank you [Time 100] for including me in this ridiculous list of brilliant and sometimes terrifying people,” he wrote. “And the gorgeous [Helen Mirren] … I thank you from the heart of my bottom for the kindest words ever.”
