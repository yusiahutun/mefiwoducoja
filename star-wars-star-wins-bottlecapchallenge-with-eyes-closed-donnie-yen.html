    Donnie Yen Wins the #Bottlecapchallenge With His Eyes Closed

    Everyone from Conor McGregor to Ellie Goulding has gotten involved. Donnie Yen could do that with his eyes closed... and he did. The #Bottlecapchallenge came to an abrupt end on Wednesday when the Star Wars star put the internet to shame by completing it blindfolded literally.

    31 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Everyone from Conor McGregor to Ellie Goulding has gotten involved.

    Donnie Yen could do that with his eyes closed... and he did.

    The #Bottlecapchallenge came to an abrupt end on Wednesday when the Star Wars star put the internet to shame by completing it blindfolded — literally.

    The martial arts legend channeled two of his most famous characters — the ass-kicking IP Man and "Rogue One" blind warrior Chirrut Îmwe — to take on the task that's been sweeping the internet for the past week.

    Measuring up the water bottle in his gym, the 55-year-old pulls a blindfold over his eyes, leaps into a jumping hook kick, unscrews the lid with the deftest of touches, before landing with a knowing smile.

    "Chirrut feeling the force + Ip man's steady aim + no plastic bottle = the universe strongest" he captioned the impressive vid.

    The challenge, which was started by hapkido champ Master Fa on Instagram seven days ago, dares followers to remove a bottlecap with a spinning kick.

    The call brought out some expected names — such as MMA superstars Conor McGregor and Max Holloway — as well as some not-so-expected ones, such as Ellie Goulding and John Mayer.

    Master Fa, who is also a Taekwondo black belt, challenged Jason Statham and McGregor in his initial post, as well as Jean-Claude Van Damme and Jackie Chan. The first two answered, the second two have yet to.

    Successful efforts ranged from the impressive (Guy Richie) to the hilarious (Diplo).

    "Since no one requested me to do this (@johnmayer ) and also since no one thinks I'm good at anything except pushing button on stage .. here is evidence that I also do karate," the DJ wrote of his awkward-but-ultimately-triumphant kick, before nominating Prince Harry, Meghan Markle, Kevin Durant and Barack Obama.

    Meanwhile, back on Master Fa's page, he was already thinking up (and completing) the next challenge — kicking the nib off a pencil.
