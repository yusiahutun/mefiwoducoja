    Taylor Swift will be 'celebrating Super Bowl outcome', celebrity psychic predicts

    Exclusive: Celebrity Tidbit sat down with a psychic medium who predicted whats to come in tonights highly anticipated Super Bowl, including exactly how Taylor Swift will be feeling during the, and most importantly, after the big game.

    27 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Exclusive: Celebrity Tidbit sat down with a psychic medium who predicted what’s to come in tonight’s highly anticipated Super Bowl, including exactly how Taylor Swift will be feeling during the, and most importantly, after the big game.

    The all-important Super Bowl LVIII will see Travis Kelce and the Kansas City Chiefs take on the San Francisco 49ers in hopes of winning their fourth title. Taylor Swift will (hopefully) be in the stands cheering him on as she ‘rushes’ back from Tokyo. Here’s how celebrity psychic Adam Norton predicts she’ll be feeling during, and after the huge game.

    Taylor Swift will be ‘celebrating’ at the end of the Super Bowl, celebrity psychic predicts

    Adam predicted it would be “emotionally high” for Taylor during the game, comparing the emotion to watching your child playing sports. “If you’ve got a kid and they’re playing sports, and a lot is riding on them to win, it’s almost going to be that kind of energy,” he says.

    However, from Norton’s predictions, it looks like the tensions may pay off, as he sees ‘celebrating’ at the end. “But I see her celebrating after it, so I almost want to say they [Kansas City Chiefs] will win,” he tells us.

    It looks like Taylor may be some sort of a good luck charm, as she’s been supporting her boyfriend and his team in the stands ever since they went public in September.

    Their cutest PDA yet came when they shared a kiss on the field after the Chiefs beat the Baltimore Ravens, securing their spot in the Super Bowl.

    Now, the all-important day is finally here. The music star has been in Tokyo this week kicking off the 2024 leg of her Eras Tour, which lead fans to fear she may not make the game.

    However, it looks like she will be there after all, and celebrity psychic Adam Norton has predicted a ‘well thought out’ entrance for the star.

    The couple set to ‘spend private time together’ following Super Bowl

    The celebrity couple have had a busy week with Travis’ Super Bowl preparations, Taylor’s Japan performances, and the Grammys.

    Unfortunately, due to Super Bowl preparations, Kelce was unable to make it to the Grammys to support Swift, but it looks like the couple will be making up for lost time after the big game.

    Trav and his family have reportedly booked an £800,000 pad described as a “private oasis on an acre”, at Lake Las Vegas for after the game, The Sun reports.

    “These two have not seen each other for a couple of weeks because of work commitments and will want some private time before the team gatherings,” an insider told the publication.

    We can’t wait to see what goes down tonight!
