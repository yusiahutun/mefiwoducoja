    Blackpink Jisoo took over the Internet recently as news of the global K-pop star dating South Korean actor Ahn Bo Hyun surfaced. While the news took K-pop fans by surprise, soon they discovered that Ahn Bo Hyun might be the ‘biggest green flag.’

    Dating in the K-pop music world has been taboo for decades, but recent years have seen a significant change as more top K-pop and K-drama stars have admitted to dating their co-stars. From EXO Chen embracing a private married life and welcoming two children to Blackpink Jisoo’s label accepting the dating rumors, K-pop has really come a long way!

    Blackpink Jisoo’s K-drama boyfriend dubbed as the ‘biggest green flag’

    It is not a mystery that dating celebrities who are constantly in the limelight is not easy and when it comes to the two most popular artists from their respective fields, things can become trickier!

    So when fans got to know details about the dating life of Ahn Bo Hyun and Jisoo, the former instantly won the hearts of many for his sweetest gestures.

    A report detailing the dating history of Jisoo and Ahn Bo Hyun revealed how the K-drama star synced his schedule to match Jisoo’s busy life when the two met in Korea, mostly at Jisoo’s home. A close source of the pair claimed: “Jisoo didn’t have many days in Korea and Ahn Bo Hyun coordinated the schedule to fit Jisoo.”

    Fans gush over Ahn Bo Hyun’s sweet gestures

    Ahn Bo Hyun’s sweet gestures are making fans swoon as one commented: “The more I read their interviews and get a feel for their core values Ahn Bohyun and Jisoo scream positive green flag couple.”

    Another fan had a similar thought and wrote: “The biggest green flag actor in K-drama-land dating with the most green flag idol in K-pop. I am so happy for Jisoo and Ahn Bohyun, Congratulations to you guys.

    “This is the definition of good people will meet good people!”

    Ahn Bo Hyun’s ‘ideal type’ in relationship matches with Jisoo perfectly

    Fans are digging up Bo Hyun’s old interviews where he talked about his ideal type of relationships.

    The 35-year-old See You In My Next Life once shared: “Respecting elders and having good manners, that’s my ideal type. Not only respecting their own parents, but being respectful to all elders in general.”

    Meanwhile, Jisoo never fails to mention her parents in any speech and once told that she would like to send flowers to her father to show the love and gratitude she has for them!

    K-pop fans have found another match made in heaven!
