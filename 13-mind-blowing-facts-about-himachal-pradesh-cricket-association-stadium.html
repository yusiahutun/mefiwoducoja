    The highest cricket stadium in the world

    The Himachal Pradesh Cricket Association Stadium, located in Dharamshala, is renowned for being the highest cricket stadium in the world. Built at an elevation of 1,457 meters (4,780 feet) above sea level, this stunning stadium offers breathtaking views of the surrounding Himalayan mountains.

    A home to picturesque surroundings

    Nestled amidst the majestic Dhauladhar range, the stadium provides a picturesque backdrop for cricket matches. The snow-capped peaks and lush greenery surrounding the stadium create a truly mesmerizing setting for both players and spectators.

    A capacity of 23,000 spectators

    The HPCA Stadium boasts a seating capacity of 23,000 people, making it one of the most notable cricket grounds in India. The sheer number of spectators that can be accommodated adds to the electrifying atmosphere during matches, creating an unforgettable experience for cricket enthusiasts.

    The Dalai Lama’s blessings

    The Dalai Lama, the spiritual leader of Tibet, has bestowed his blessings upon the Himachal Pradesh Cricket Association Stadium. His presence during the inauguration of the stadium in 2003 added a sense of spiritual significance and grandeur to the venue.

    An eco-friendly stadium

    The HPCA Stadium is designed with a strong emphasis on environmental sustainability. It incorporates environmentally friendly features such as rainwater harvesting systems and solar panels, showcasing a commitment to preserving the natural beauty of the region.

    A venue for international and domestic cricket matches

    The stadium has hosted numerous international matches, including Test matches, One Day Internationals, and T20 Internationals. It has also been a popular venue for domestic cricket tournaments, attracting teams from all over the country to compete on its hallowed turf.

    A curator’s paradise

    The unique climate and natural surroundings of Dharamshala provide an ideal setting for maintaining the cricket pitch. The stadium’s pitch curator has the rewarding task of ensuring a perfect playing surface for the players, making it a true paradise for cricket enthusiasts.

    A challenging elevation for players

    The high altitude of the HPCA Stadium poses a unique challenge for both local and visiting players. The thin air and lack of oxygen can affect ball movement and stamina, adding to the excitement and unpredictability of matches hosted at this remarkable venue.

    A favorite training destination for international teams

    Due to its stunning location and state-of-the-art facilities, the Himachal Pradesh Cricket Association Stadium has become a sought-after destination for international teams to conduct their training camps. Many teams have utilized the stadium’s excellent facilities to prepare for important cricket tournaments.
