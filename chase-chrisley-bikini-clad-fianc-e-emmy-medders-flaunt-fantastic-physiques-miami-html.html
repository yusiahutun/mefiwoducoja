    16 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Chase Chrisley was spotted having some fun in the sun with his fiancée Emmy Medders in Miami, Florida, on Saturday.

    The Growing Up Chrisley alumnus and Medders, both 26, showed off their stunning physiques on the beach and engaged in some PDA.

    The pair jetted off for their relaxing vacation to get away from Chris' family drama, a month after his reality TV star parents began their combined 19-year prison sentence.

    Todd, 53, and Julie Chrisley, 49, are serving their sentence for bank fraud and tax evasion.

    Chase and Emmy soaked up the sun and took a dip in the ocean away from all of the drama.

    Vacation time: Chase Chrisley was seen at the beach in Miami on Saturday with his fiancée Emmy Medders

    Beach babe: The pair flaunted their beach bodies while on vacation. He wore red Nike swim trunks and she rocked a lustrous reddish-brown bikini

    Fun in the sun: The newly engaged couple were spotted engaging in some PDA

    The beach beauty wore a reddish-brown triangle bikini, exposing her toned stomach and long legs.

    She wore her blonde hair pulled up in a messy bun and a pair of sunglasses on her makeup-free face.

    Chase sported a pair of bright red board shorts and kept his shiny silver watch on his wrist.

    The newly engaged couple went for a swim in the clear blue ocean and pulled each other in for a sweet kiss.

    While on their vacation, Emmy shared several shots of what the loved-up couple has been doing.

    She posted photos of the greenery on the resort and a selfie with her friend Anna as they held drinks while outside.

    Emmy also shared pictures from her nightlife activities with Chase, showing the two heading out to Carbone Restaurant and out on the streets of Miami.

    The lovebirds got engaged in October of last year when Chase popped the question on the field at First Horizon Park in Tennessee.

    He shared the photos of the proposal with his 1.3 million Instagram followers and wrote in the caption: 'I am the luckiest man in the world, being able to call you mine forever is a blessing straight from God.

    'You are the sunshine when it’s dark, you are the rain when it’s dry. I love you more than life itself and can’t wait to live forever with you! To you my love! Thank you for taking me FOREVER!! @emmymedders'

    The adorable pair will have to plan their wedding without Chase's parents, Todd and Julie.

    Julie turned herself in last month at the Federal Medical Center in Lexington, Kentucky, where she will serve seven years.

    Cutest couple: Emmy posted several photos of their vacation to her Instagram 

    Friend time: The beauty shared a photo with one of her friends Anna Smith while they held drinks outside

    Night out: The 26-year-olds had a night out at Carbone Restaurant 

    The family: Chase's parents, Todd and Julie, just started their 19-year combined prison sentence last month; Julie, Savannah, Chase, and Todd Chrisley in 2018

    Scandal: Todd and Julie turned themselves in after committing bank fraud and tax evasion; the Chrisley family for season six of Chrisley Knows Best 

    Later that same morning, Todd was snapped surrendering at the Federal Prison Camp in Pensacola, Florida, where he will spend 12 years. 

    The couple will also have a 16-month probation period for both upon their release, though they are currently working on appealing their case.

    Meanwhile, Chase's sister Savannah recently revealed that the family might partake in a new show after Chrisley Knows Best and its spinoffs Growing Up Chrisley and Love Limo have all been canceled following their parents' conviction.

    'Tons of production companies have reached out and networks that want to do a show,' Savannah claimed on the latest episode of her podcast, adding, 'At the end of the day, we're not going to allow this to stop us from moving forward.'
