    Table of Biography

    Michel Duval is a Mexican actor, songwriter, singer, composer, and model. Michel Duval is best known for La Rosa de Guadalupe, Senora Acero, and Atrevete a Sonar. Likewise, Michel is famous for Puno Limpio (2019), Roy EI Mago (2018), and Deadly Class (2018).

    Early Life and Education

    Michel Duval was born on 10th February 1994 as Michel Dussauge. As of 2022, his age is 28 years old and he was born under the zodiac sign Aquarius. Similarly, he belongs to the Mexican nationality and holds Hispanic ethnicity. Michel is the son of a mother Consuelo Duval who is also an actress and comedian. Likewise, he is the brother of actress Paly Duval.

    Talking about his educational status, Michel studied at the Centro de Educacion Artistica of Televisa, and later he studied at the American Academy of Dramatic Arts. Also, he attended New York City’s The New York Conservatory for Dramatic Arts in 2010. Michel began acting at a very young, and he discovered his passion for art.

    Career

    Michel Duval is a Mexican actor, singer, songwriter, and model. Duval made his debut in the soap opera Lo Impersonal, after appearing in Atrevete a Sonar. He also starred in several shows in Mexico. Likewise, Michel got a role in the MTV Latin America series ‘Ultimo, where he played Miguel Angel Valdez in the pilot episode. After the success of Ultimo, Duval moved to Los Angeles, California to pursue a new career.

    Similarly, in theatre, he appeared in Romeo and Juliet and Pericles in New York City. Likewise, he played the role of Salvador Acero, son of Sara, in the second season of the Mexican television series ‘Senora Acero with Blanca Soto. In the year 2016, this personality launched his first solo album.

    In 2020, he played the role of Marco Antonio Marquez Ochelli de la Garza in the television series ‘Herederos por accidente’. Further, in November 2018, he announced that he was leaving Senora Acero to announce his new upcoming show Deadly Class which will air on Syfy in January 2019.
