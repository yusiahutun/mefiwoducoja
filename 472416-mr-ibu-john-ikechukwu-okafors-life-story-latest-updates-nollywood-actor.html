    Nollywood has seen tremendous growth over the years. It boasts of some renowned actors who have touched hearts and made their name over the years. John Ikechukwu Okafor, famously known as Mr. Ibu, is one of the talented actors who have made their marks in the sand of time. There is excitement that comes with watching a Nollywood film and seeing Mr. Ibu in it.

    Mr. Ibu is a famous Nigerian actor and comedian. Thanks to his humourous acting, he has stood out from the other actors. He rose to prominence in 2004 when he was cast to play the role of Ibu in Mr Ibu and Mr Ibu in London. Since then, he has featured in hundreds of films. Some of his notable roles include Toronto Connection (2007), Ibu in Prison (2006), and Police Recruit (2003).

    How much is John Ikechukwu Okafor's net worth?

    Okafor is ranked among the highest-paid comic actors in Nollywood. His net worth is estimated at $4.2 million. As a veteran actor, he pockets a significant sum from every movie appearance. Mr. Ibu also earns an impressive sum as a businessman and from endorsements.

    Mr. Ibu's house is a testimony that he has amassed a fortune from his successful career. He lives in a multi-million mansion located in Lagos, Nigeria.

    Is John Ikechukwu Okafor married?

    The actor is happily married to Stella Maris Okafor. His wife is an actress and model. They dated for a while before getting married in 2005. Mr. Ibu and his wife are still together. They are the proud parents of three children. The names of their children are Emmanuel, Chelsea, and Jay Jay. He also has other children, including Jasmine Okafor, from his past relationships.

    Was Mr. Ibu poisoned?

    Mr. Ibu's health was a trending topic in March 2022. The actor survived a near-death experience after he was poisoned at an event in Nigeria. After being poisoned, his stomach grew so big that he could not see his legs.

    Mr. Ibu was admitted to a private hospital, where he was treated. He was discharged after spending a few days in the hospital. The comedian attributes his being alive to God.
