    Julien Farel Express Facial

    Time: 25 minutes

    Treatment: This facial by Natura Bissé includes chemical or physical exfoliation (your call), targeted moisturizing, and a relaxing facial massage. 

    Cost: $125

    Where: Julien Farel Restore Salon & Spa in New York City, julienfarel.com

    CryoSphere Cryo Facial

    Time: 15 minutes

    Treatment: A technician blasts your face with cold air (think -100 degrees) to tighten pores and stimulate blood circulation. You'll notice smoother skin instantly—but you'll have to keep coming back to sustain the effect.

    Cost: $85

    Where: The CryoSphere at The Alchemist's Kitchen and Higher Dose, thecryosphere.com

    The Peel Bar by BeautyRx

    Time: 15 minutes

    Treatment: After cleansing and toning, a technician applies a 40% glycolic acid peel for two minutes. Once rinsed, you're sent on your way with some moisturizer and sunscreen. It's an ideal monthly treatment for acne-prone or uneven skin.

    Cost: $50

    Where: Butterfly Studio Salon, butterflystudiosalon.com

    Skin Laundry Laser & Light Facial

    Time: 15 minutes

    Treatment: Using a combination of a YAG laser and IPL light treatment, this quick laser-based facial leaves skin a little softer and brighter with each session. And don't worry: your skin won't be peely or red afterward. 

    Cost: $65

    Where: Skin Laundry locations, skinlaundry.com

    Dermalogica MicroZone

    Time: 20 minutes

    Treatment: These 20-minute sessions target your biggest skin problems: dryness, acne, fine lines, or dull skin. A technician will extract, scrub, smooth, and hydrate whatever your skin may have and then send you on your way—with no frills or time-sucking extras.

    Cost: $50

    Where:  Dermalogica SoHo, dermalogica.com, and Body Factory Skin Care, bodyfactoryskincare.com

    Silver Mirror Signature Facial

    Time: 30 minutes

    Treatment: This gives you all the benefits of a classic facial—extractions, oxygen therapy, exfoliation, cleansing—stuffed into one half-hour session.

    Cost: $75

    Where: Silver Mirror, silvermirror.com

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    Heyday 30 Minute Facial

    Time: 30 minutes

    Treatment: If you're not into extractions and just need your skin to look less blah, the classic Heyday facial will get your skin smooth and glowing. It's perfect before a big night out. If you need a little more exfoliation or deep pore cleansing, upgrade to the 50-minute facial or add-on microdermabrasion or a peel (for an extra 35 bucks). 

    Cost: $60

    Where: Heyday locations, thinkheyday.com
