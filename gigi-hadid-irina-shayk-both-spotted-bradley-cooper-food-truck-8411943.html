    Gigi Hadid and Irina Shayk Were Both Spotted Supporting Bradley Cooper at His NYC Food Truck

    07 Apr 2024, 00:00

    While Bradley Cooper was certainly feeling the love from New Yorkers while manning the grill of his new food truck, Danny & Coop’s Cheesesteak, on Wednesday, it seems he also had support from two extra-special guests: his rumored flame, Gigi Hadid, and his ex-girlfriend, Irina Shayk.

    According to customers on the scene, per E! News, both Hadid and Shayk were spotted at Cooper’s truck at different points in the day, with Gigi taking her snack to-go after dropping by with Laura Dern (who’s rumored to be dating Cooper’s business partner, Danny DiGiampietro) and Irina arriving an hour later to chat with her ex on the sidewalk as he took a break from cooking. 

    Calling on her best winter-ready OOTD for the occasion, Hadid — who’s been linked to Cooper since early October — sported a shearling-lined denim jacket paired with a black T-shirt, matching wide-leg sweatpants, and a navy blue New York Yankees baseball cap. A black leather handbag, gold chain necklace, and oval-shaped sunglasses rounded out her look, and she wore her blonde hair down in soft waves. 

    For her part, Shayk (who shares 6-year-old daughter Lea with the actor) opted to bundle up a bit more during the outing by layering a black Nike puffer coat over a coordinating black shirt and pants. The model accessorized with shield sunglasses, black leather shoes, and headphones, and she wore her brunette hair down straight with a middle part. 

    Although Bradley and Irina dated for several years before calling it quits in 2017, there isn’t any bad blood between the pair — in fact, Shayk was actually the one to set her ex up with Hadid.

    “Gigi was introduced to Bradley through Irina [Shayk] and their mutual friends in the industry,” a source told The Messenger back in Oct. “Gigi and Irina have been close over the years while working together, and Gigi and Bradley have hung out before, but it has always been friendly in social settings. Gigi and Bradley were in touch recently, and were bonding over their daughters, when Bradley asked her out.”
