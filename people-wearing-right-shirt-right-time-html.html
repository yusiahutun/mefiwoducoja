    When it comes to meeting a celebrity, most of us would hope to be dressed for the occasion, however these people hit the jack pot.

    A hilarious online gallery captures people who coincidentally wore the right t-shirt at the right time.

    In many cases superfans just happened to be wearing clothing dedicated to their favourite films when they bumped into their idols. 

    In one snap a man is pictured wearing a Basic Instinct t-shirt while posing next to the movie's star Sharon Stone, and in another Bill Murray stands beside a fan in a top printed with his face. 

    Other fans let the chance to meet their heroes pass them by with a Led Zeppelin fan wearing a band t-shirt dozing unaware that John Paul Jones is posing next to him.

    Elsewhere coincidences were less happy with convicts arrested in t-shirts brandishing logos such as 'Go To Jail'.
