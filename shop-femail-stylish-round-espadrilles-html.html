    With today being the start of June, it's officially time to break out your espadrilles. The raffia soled shoes are classic, never go out of style and can be worn with virtually anything.

    Pippa Middleton, 33, Alessandra Ambrosio, 36, Jennifer Aniston, 48, and Troian Bellisario, 31, have all stepped out recently in different variations of the summer staple.  

    From wedge styles to slip-ons, sandals and flats, FEMAIL rounds up the 24 chicest pairs to shop now. 

    WEDGES

    Vacay vibes: Pippa Middleton, 33, sported black wedge espadrilles by Castañer as she honneymooned in in Sydney, Australia yesterday

    Classic options: Left: Zara wedges, $49.90, zara.com. Center: Castañer wedges, $85, net-a-porter.com. Right: Sole Society wedges, $94.95, solesociety.com

    Cool neutrals: Left: Saint Laurent wedges, $298, fwrd.com. Right: Steve Madden wedges, $89.95, stevemadden.com

    Try stripes: Lanvin wedges, $316, matchesfashion.com

    SLIP-ONS

    Off-duty style: Alessandra Ambrosio, 36, donned black leather espadrilles by Chanel as she hit the streets of Los Angeles 

    Summer staple: Left: Soludos slip-ons, $39.75, theoutnet.com. Center: H&M slip-ons, $17.99, hm.com. Right: Stuart Weitzman slip-ons, $398, stuartweitzman.com

    Two-toned: Left: Zara slip-ons, $29.90, zara.com. Right: Paul Andrew slip-ons, $247, louisaviaroma.com

    Pretty print: Isabel Marant Etoil slip-ons, $79, barneys.com

    SANDALS

    Stylish star: Jennifer Aniston, 48, donned Soludos espadrille sandals as she ran errands around West Hollywood, CA recently

    Strappy details: Left: Proenza Schouler sandals, $255, ssense.com. Center: River Island sandals, $44, riverisland.com. Right: Zara sandals, $69.90, zara.com

    Trendy slides: Left: Soludos sandals, $89, revolve.com. Right: Sam Edelman sandals, $54, net-a-porter.com

    Showstoppers: Christian Louboutin sandals, $795, net-a-porter.com

    Summer style: Troian Bellisario, 31, paired her light blue romper with Viscata lace up espadrilles

    Lace it up: Left: Castañer flats, $55, net-a-porter.com. Center: H&M flats, $29.99, hm.com. Right: Frye flats, $99, gilt.com

    Back to basics: Left: Steve Madden flats, $79.95, stevemadden.com. Right: Aquazzura flats, $357, aquazzura.com
