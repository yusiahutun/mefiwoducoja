    Dove Cameron showed off her edgy side while flashing her cleavage in a bra under a satin suit as she stepped out in New York City on Wednesday. 

    The Boyfriend hitmaker, 27, who recently turned heads as she stopped by Live! With Kelly And Ryan, has been promoting the upcoming second season of Apple TV+'s Schmigadoon! which is set to premiere on April 5. 

    The former Disney channel star donned a pair of loose-fitting, bronze slacks that were made of a sleek satin material. 

    The Descendants actress opted to wear a matching blazer on top that was secured with a button at the front and also contained a plunging neckline. 

    Dove chose to not add a shirt underneath, and for a stylish flare, simply wore a skimpy, dark brown bralette underneath.

    She slipped into a pair of open-toed, black heels that were partially concealed by the long hem of her slacks. 

    Her dark locks were slicked back into a ponytail, allowing the long front bangs to be parted in the middle to border the sides of her face in elegant curls. 

    The actress kept her accessories simple and minimalistic for the outing, and added a dainty pair of silver earrings. 

    Dove also chose to throw on a pair of green-rimmed sunglasses that added a pop of color to her overall ensemble. 

    Her makeup was glammed up, and comprised of a layer of black mascara to her long lashes along with a smoky, bronze shadow around her eyes. 

    A light pink blush was added to better accentuate her cheekbones, while a rosy pink satin tint was worn on her lips for a finishing touch. 

    The beauty flashed a cheerful smile as she paused for a quick photo session while strolling along a sidewalk located in the Midtown area of the bustling city. 

    Cameron recently moved to the big apple, which she confirmed while stopping by The Tonight Show With Jimmy Fallon earlier on Monday.

    The singer also revealed to the host how her hit 2022 track, Boyfriend, was also almost not released. 

    After stating that she had just wrapped up a small tour at the time, Dove added, 'My voice was shot. And the very next day, my label was like, 'You have to start working on your album.' 

    'So we got into the studio immediately and we wrote Boyfriend in like two hours, and I was like, 'This is bad.' And I recorded it in like 20 minutes, and we just put it away, and I didn't really think anything of it.' 

    'And then it turned out to be, like, the thing that kind of gave me a sort of surprise second career.'  The star released a snippet of the track on TikTok earlier last year, which soon went viral. 

    She also teased fans that, 'I'm actually working on my debut album pretty much every day now, which I'm very very excited about,' and added that there will be a 'few surprise guest stars' on the album. 

    While she kept some of the details of its contents to herself, Dove revealed that an upcoming single that she is also working on is a collaboration with Diplo, but did not hint at any release date. 

    While focusing on her music career, the beauty has also been working hard in front of the camera, and recently showed off photos from her latest Carolina Herrera Spring/Summer 2023 campaign on her main Instagram page on Monday. 

    Latest campaign: Dove recently showed off photos from her latest Carolina Herrera Spring/Summer 2023 campaign

    Bold look: The actress and singer opted to wear a short blonde wig and bleached her eyebrows blonde for the luxurious shoot 

    Striking a pose: The Boyfriend hitmaker had the chance to model various outfits from the brand's Spring/Summer 2023 collection 

    According to Elle, the designs came from the creative mind of the brand's current designer, Wes Gordon, while the shoot was photographed by Elizaveta Porodina. 

    'It’s such a dream when you get to be friends with an iconic designer who’s the head of an iconic fashion house,' Dove informed the outlet. 

    'I look at fashion designers and photographers the same way I look at composers. [They’re] people who do things that are so magical and otherworldly. They’re geniuses,' she further continued. 

    The photos were full of colors, and the actress added that Wes Gorgon, 'wanted me to bring as much of myself to the shoot as I possibly could, and I was grateful that he trusted me to do that. So much of this day was just playing and creating shapes, colors, tones, and textures.' 

    In regards to her bold look for the campaign shoot, which ditched her brunette locks for a short blonde wig and also bleached her eyebrows, Cameron stated, 'I looked like a completely different person,' and humorously added that she looked, 'like my 8-year-old self.' 

    In regards to the special collaboration and how she has become the brand's 'new muse,' Dove expressed, 'To be integrally connected to people who make this their life, this is their talent, this is their joy, it’s always really special to me.'

    She then sent her gratitude to the photographer 'for capturing these surrealistic moments in Paris. such an honor.' 

    Dove has also been promoting the upcoming second season of her Apple TV+ show, Schmigadoon!, which premieres later next month on April 5. 

    The star-studded cast also includes SNL alum Cecily Strong, Keegan-Michael Key, Ariana DeBose, and Alan Cumming. 

    'Everybody has such a good time together, and the chemistry is so real and honest, and they're just the best people around,' Cameron said her cast members on the Today show earlier this month. 

    In the second season, Dove portrays the character of nightclub performer, Jenny Banks, in the anthology musical comedy. While the first season focused on a parody of musicals from the 1940s and 1950s, the second will have a satirical take on 1960s and 1970s musicals.
