    Spring is finally here, but the fluctuating temperature suggests otherwise; we’ve officially entered the awkward period where chunky knits leave you sweating yet breezy dresses aren’t quite cutting it just yet. The solution: lightweight cardigans, of course. I don’t know about you, but airy sweaters have already become my spring uniform, and I’m taking to Amazon to revamp my everyday collection. To welcome the season in style, I sifted through the retailer’s selection to narrow down the top six under-$50 cardigans worth shopping.  

    The following picks are sourced from Amazon’s best-sellers chart, a real-time ranking of the items shoppers are buying most. Below, meet your new favorite styles starting at just $18. (Yes, $18.) You’ll find open-front sweaters to throw over any spring ensemble, plus chic button-up cardigans that are bound to make a statement this season. Regardless of which selects catch your eye, be sure to grab them ASAP; they’re customer-loved for a reason and bound to sell out quickly.  

    Best Amazon Spring Cardigans 

    Prettygarden Lightweight Knit Cardigan

    What better sweater to shop first than Amazon’s hottest new cardigan release? The retailer just dropped this Prettygarden style, and it’s already trending (for good reason). The find is made from a super lightweight waffle-knit material, and it has cool short sleeves and a boxy fit reminiscent of your favorite tee. My personal favorite touch, the piece has gold statement buttons for an extra elevated look. Pair it with jeans or shorts, or dress it up with a skirt and heels — the styling options are endless. While you can never go wrong with pastel pink for spring, the sweater is available in seven neutral colorways, too.  

    Lillusory Buttoned Crewneck Sweater

    Another cart-worthy pick, this Lillusory sweater is now marked down to $47 from its usual $60 price tag. It’s made from a polyester blend that will keep you cozy for chilly spring mornings yet cool enough once temperatures rise. The cardigan has a classic crewneck, relaxed fit, and chic gold button details. Plus, it can easily be layered over practically anything in your wardrobe. According to its rave reviews, shoppers are big fans of the versatile find. One customer described it as “amazing quality,” adding that they “get a ton of compliments on it.”      

    Amazon Essentials Lightweight Crewneck Cardigan

    At $18, this Amazon Essentials cardigan is nearly impossible to pass up. In spite of its budget-friendly cost, the staple piece looks and feels “much more expensive” than it is, according to one reviewer. It has a timeless appearance thanks to its crew-neckline and flattering, snug fit. The sweater is made from a blend of cotton and polyester for a soft-yet-airy-feel that’s ideal for transitional weather. Shop the wardrobe basic in 27 colorways including easy-to-wear neutrals, bold brights, and spring-ready pastels. Still on the fence? Take it from one shopper who deemed it “perfect for spring,” adding that it’s so soft it “feels like a comforting hug.”  

    Run to Amazon to snag these lightweight cardigans from $18 just in time for spring.
