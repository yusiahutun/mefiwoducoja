    1. Who directed The Commitments?
    The Commitments was directed by Alan Parker, who expertly brought Roddy Doyle’s novel to life on the big screen.

    2. Is The Commitments based on a true story?
    While The Commitments is a work of fiction, it authentically captures the spirit and music scene of working-class Dublin in the 1980s.

    Yes, the actors in The Commitments received extensive training to perform their own music, adding to the authenticity of the film.
