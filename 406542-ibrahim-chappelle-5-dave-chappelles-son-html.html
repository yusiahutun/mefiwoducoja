However, there are some things about Ibrahim worth knowing about, and they include:

    David Chappelle married Elaine Mendoza in 2001, and the couple is blessed with three children. Their first child Sulayman is a son born in 2001. That makes him around 19 years. Ibrahim is the second born. The youngest in the family is Sonal, who is also the only daughter.
