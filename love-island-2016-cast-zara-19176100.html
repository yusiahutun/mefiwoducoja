    Love Island cast revealed: A webcam babe, Danny Cipriani's ex and Gaz Beadle's bouncer

    07 Oct 2024, 00:00

    The hit ITV show looks set to dominate the headlines with producers having lined up a host of wannabe Z-list names.

    From Miss Great Britain Zara Holland to Danny Cipriani's ex, one thing is for certain – this year's series is seriously hot.

    Here we check out the full list of names set to keep you glued to your TV screens.

    The Girls:

    Malin Andersson:

    Bedford-based Malin has previously tried her hand at TV dating when she appeared on Take Me Out a couple of years ago.

    However, despite her stunning good looks the 23-year-old failed to get a date – something she claims is because she is too picky.

    But it isn't all bad news, as Malin previously caught the eye of Geordie Shore's Aaron Chalmers and serial bed-hopper Scotty T.

    Zara Holland:

    At just 20 years old, Zara is the youngest of the hopefuls but that doesn't mean she isn't out to win.

    The model is currently Miss Great Britain – a title previously held by fellow contestant Sophie Gradon.

    As previously revealed by Daily Star Online, the blonde beauty is the ex of last year's hopeful Max Morley.

    The duo enjoyed a brief fling following his split from MTV regular Charlotte Crosby – though awkwardly Zara claims she's been single for two years.

    Cara De La Hoyde:

    Sex worker Cara is a regular on adult TV channel Studio 66.

    The 25-year-old may be one of the show's kinkiest stars to date.

    Not only has she posed for more topless shoots than we care to remember but she also describes herself a "good twerker".

    She previously told Sunday Sport how she "craves outdoor sex because she loves the thrill of being caught".

    Sophie Gradon:

    Sophie, 30, may be the oldest babe on the island but that doesn't mean she is past it.

    The brunette beauty has been branded a Kelly Brook lookalike and even bedded the model's ex Danny Cipriani.

    Based in Newcastle, the former Miss Great Britain has developed a reputation for being "wild and daft", often spotted on the town knocking back the booze.

    However, it seems she likes a bad boy, admitting she "often picks players" over nice guys.

    Well if you're looking for a player, Sophie, you've come to the right place.

    Olivia Buckland:

    Essex babe Olivia says she is determined to find love on the show, admitting being single is boring.

    "It was good at the start but now I want someone to snuggle up with on the sofa," she said.

    Yeah, if you are looking for that kind of guy Liv, you may be better off at your local library than Love Island.

    The Boys:

    Scott Thomas:

    Scott Thomas is the brother of Corrie regular Ryan Thomas, though looks set to carve out a name for himself.

    And by the sounds of it, he plans to use his buff bod to make waves.

    Over the past few months Scott, 27, has religiously been hitting the gym – going from toned lad to ripped hunk.

    Scott says he doesn't like girls who go for money and status so like Olivia, we can't help but wonder if he has come to the wrong place.

    Tom Powell:

    Welsh hunk Tom is a personal trainer and bar man.

    But when he's not pulling pints, he works for Gaz Beadle as his bodyguard.

    He was hired by the womaniser to do his "personal security" during filming of Geordie Shore.

    Nathan Massey:

    Cheeky chap Nathan may look innocent, but behind his "butter wouldn't melt" smile he's a bad boy at heart.

    And by the sounds of it he isn't too picky when it comes to girls… or should we say women.

    He previously bedded a woman in her 50s during a wild night out in Bournemouth.

    Though if any of the Love Island babes are hoping for a night of passion, then they may be disappointed.

    Nathan admits his romp only lasted a couple of minutes – though they were the "best two minutes of her life".

    Rykard Jenkins:

    Kent-based Rykard may be the cringiest contestant of the year.

    He claims he is going on the show as he is "looking for Summer Loving like Danny Zuko".

    As for his favourite body part on a girl, Rykard says he loves a good set of legs.

    Though only if they are not hairy.

    Daniel Lukakis:

    Model Dan claims to be a romantic at heart, admitting he loves nothing more than wining and dining a girl.

    He landed his first big break in the industry when David Gandy "scouted him" in the gym.

    Girls, watch this space.

    Javi Shephard:

    Javi is a 27-year-old surveyor – and he's definitely got an eye for the ladies.

    He's certainly not shy, even boasting about his "incredibly large tongue" which he is happy to whip out at any occasion.

    We're not going to tell you what he claims it can do – but we reckon you can fill in the gaps.

    He is a self-confessed serial dater, using Tinder, Facebook and good old fashion nights out to pull girls.

    He claims to go on dates at least once or twice a week but has never been in love.
