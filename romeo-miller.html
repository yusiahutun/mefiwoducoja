    Professional Life

    At the beginning of  Romeo Miller’s career, he began to sing in the records of his father Master P. Later on, he released his first debut album ‘Lil’ Romeo’, in 2001. He released his second album ‘Game Time’, in 2002.

    Moving on, he ventured into acting in films like ‘Max Keeble’s Big Move’. In the 2003 movie, ‘Honey’, he starred opposite Jessica Alba.

    Caption: Romeo Miller in an event. Source: Getty Image

    In the year 2004, he came up with his album ‘Romeoland’. Besides that, he collaborated with his brother Valentino Miller and his cousins, Lil’ D, Willie J, and C-Los, and formed the group ‘Rich Boyz’. In 2005, the group released its debut album ‘Young Ballers: The Hood Been Good to Us’. Likewise, he released his next album, ‘Greatest Hits’, in 2006.

    He again came up with more albums named, ‘Lottery’ and ‘God’s Gift’. T. Therefore, he joined his father and released the album, ‘Hip Hop History, in 2007. He started working on his fourth album, ‘Gumbo Station, in the year 2008. In addition, he proposed to change its name to ‘The College Boy’ and finally to ‘I Am No Limit.’

    More About Career

    He released a song entitled, ‘Get Low LP’ to promote ‘I Am No Limit’, in 2009. Thus, the collaboration album ‘Spring Beak’ was released under this label. Later, he released the album, ‘I Am No Limit’, in 2011. With his father and uncle, he went on a promotional tour titled, ‘No Limit Forever International’.

    Hence, he founded a new group by the name, ‘RESQ3’ and they intend to release an album shortly, in 2012. Moving on,  the group has released the single ‘The Only One’. There was an announcement made that he was working on a new clothing line named ‘Rome Everything, in 2013.

    Career Highlights

    He came up with 2 mixtapes named ‘We All We Got’ and ‘Hustlin, in 2015. In January 2016, he came out with his seventh mixtape titled ‘Fighting Monsters’. Moreover, he released his fourth studio album ‘Hidden Treasure’ in December 2019.

    He has started in films like ‘God’s Gift’, ‘Uncle P’, ‘Down and Distance’, ‘Jumping the Broom’, ‘Madea’s Witness Protection’, ‘Frog Kingdom’, ‘Could This Be Love’, and ‘Brotherly Love’.

    He joined the cast of the reality television series ‘Growing Up Hip Hop, in 2016. Besides that, he has also appeared in TV  shows like ‘Hip Hop Squares,’ Famous in Love,’ ‘Ex on the Beach,’ ‘Tales,’ and ‘Celebrity Watch Party.’

    He landed a role as ‘Matthew Wright’ in the fantasy drama film ‘A Miracle Before Christmas’, in 2022. Later on, he was also seen in the Troy Scott-directed television film ‘Wrath: A Seven Deadly Sins Story.’ In addition, he starred as ‘EJ’ in the musical romance drama film ‘Christmas Angel’, in 2023.
