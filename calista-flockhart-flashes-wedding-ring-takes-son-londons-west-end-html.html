    Most newlyweds are inseparable in the first days after their wedding, but Calista Flockhart was without new husband Harrison Ford as she arrived in London this week.

    But the star wasn't completely alone, she had her nine-year-old son Liam for company, and both enjoyed a night at the theatre.

    Quality time: Calista Flockhart and son Liam leave London's Ambassadors Theatre after watching the musical Stomp

    Bling: Flockhart's wedding ring was on display for the first time after her June 15 nuptials with Harrison Ford

    The 45-year-old Brothers and Sisters actress and her adopted son dined at the Ivy before going to see the musical theatre Stomp, at the Ambassador.

    The actress, who shot to fame on U.S television show Ally McBeal, flashed a glimpse of her wedding ring as she stepped out in a simple navy blue dress, black cardigan and ballet flats.

    Flockhart wed 67-year-old Indiana Jones star Ford at the Governor's mansion in Santa Fe, New Mexico, where the actor is currently filming Cowboys and Aliens.

    Ford took time out from filming for the wedding - his third - but there was no time for a honeymoon as he had to return to work on the sci-fi western.

    The pair had been dating for eight years and became engaged on Valentine's Day last year.

    According to People magazine, the bride wore a white sundress and the groom put on his best pair of Wrangler jeans.

    Liam was the ring bearer for the ceremony, which was officiated by the Chief Justice of New Mexico, Charles W. Daniels.

    Newlywed: Flockhart looked casual yet chic in a navy dress and ballet flats
