    Katherine lives with her husband Andrew Levitas and their children Aaliyah and Xander

      A Katherine Jenkins album makes for classic listening at Christmas. With her beautiful operatic voice and glamorous outfits, it doesn't get much more festive. In fact, the star is on our screens tonight for her show on BBC Two at 7:10 pm. But the Welsh singer, 43, isn't just the owner of a fabulous voice, but also a fabulous home.

      Fans will recall that the star performed from her home during the Covid-19 lockdown to boost peoples' spirits but now she shares glimpses of her home with her 133 thousand Instagram followers. 

      One room, where Katherine had previously dressed up for her weekly performances, features opulent décor with high ceilings and polished flooring with an antique rug at the centre. The 43-year-old has a black piano ready for her performances, while a cushioned black leather bench sits in front of an open fireplace.

      A huge arched mirror with a gold frame hangs on the wall behind the mirror, and Katherine also has a painting with a gilded frame on display at the back of the room. Here are a few of our favourite snaps of Katherine's stunning home.

      Katherine's festive area for 2023

      Katherine has shared a glimpse into her festive at-home set-up for 2023. Taking to Instagram, the 'Jealous of the Angels' singer showed off her Christmassy living room which features an old-fashioned fireplace topped with a leafy garland, warm fairy lights, and candles.

      The room also features a classy neutral rug and a low coffee table where the star, who was spotted lighting the fire with an ultra-trendy bow in her hair, had laid out a delicious spread.

      Katherine's grand piano

      As you might expect, the songstress' home has a stunning black grand piano. The room boasting the impressive instrument has a cream and gold colour scheme and leads through to another reception room with another painting and elegant lights hanging on the walls.

      Katherine shared a look at the luxurious room when she celebrated her 40th birthday, showing how the entire top of the piano had been covered with birthday cards.

      Katherine's sitting room

      Another living room seating area has white wooden paneling on the walls, and a purple and green tartan armchair, where Katherine sat to record a video. A circular wooden side table has been topped with a jug of daffodils and pewter rabbit ornament.

      Katherine's kitchen

      Speaking to HELLO! in 2017, Katherine said her kitchen is her favourite room in the house – and we can see why! The mum-of-two sat at a breakfast bar with black worktops in this photo, with open shelving displaying books and ornaments behind her, and windows looking out to the garden.

      Katherine's front door

      Katherine performed on her front doorstep during the coronavirus lockdown, showing her huge double front doors which are painted black to contrast with the cream painted house.

      Katherine's garden

      Katherine previously shared a live performance from her garden showcasing her expansive lawn that is surrounded by hedges and has border areas filled with plants and shrubs.
