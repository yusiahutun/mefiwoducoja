    Guy Cecil is known as a notable American leader and political strategist, currently holds the position of Chairman of Priorities USA Action, an independent political action committee, and also founded Miles Strategies. Prior to this, he served as the former chief of staff to Colorado Sen. Michael Bennet and was the political director for Hillary Clinton.

    Early Life

    Born on July 1, 1974, in Hialeah, Florida, Guy Cecil is currently 50 years old as of 2023. He has never spoken about his parents or childhood. In terms of education, he completed his high school education in 1993 at NMB (North Miami Beach) Senior High School, Florida, United States. He then attended the University of Florida from 1992 to 1996, where he earned a Bachelor’s degree in Political Science.

    Guy Cecil Career

    Guy Cecil started his professional journey as a high school teacher at Northwest Christian Academy situated in Miami, Florida. Alongside his teaching job, he was also serving as a minister at Northwest Baptist Church. Afterward, he shifted to Boston, Massachusetts, and participated in Tom Keane Jr.’s campaign for the 8th Congressional district of Massachusetts. Moreover, he previously served as a member of Dewey Square Group, an organization that aids corporate advocacy campaigns.

    He managed Mark Pryor’s (D) U.S. Senate campaign in Arkansas in 2002 and served as the campaign manager for Erskine Bowles’ (D) Senate campaign in North Carolina in 2004. In addition, he worked as a consultant for Gen. Wesley Clark’s (D) presidential campaign in the same year.
