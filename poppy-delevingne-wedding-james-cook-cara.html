      Make-up artist to the stars Charlotte Tilbury has shared a series of pictures showing Poppy Delevingne's bridal beauty look.Charlotte, who was charged with helping model Poppy look every inch the blushing bride for her wedding, posted photos of her getting ready for her big day at Claridge's Hotel.One photo showed the 27-year-old in her bespoke Chanel couture gown, which was adorned with lace flowers and jewels, holding a make-up compact while loooking into the camera lens.

      "@poppydelevingne looking radiant on her wedding day," Charlotte captioned the picture. "Congratulations darling! xx #gettingreadywithCT"Poppy kept her make-up simple, opting for a smokey eye effect and a slick of pale pink lipstick.The socialite's look was evident in Charlotte's close-up photos, one of which showed the bride posing with her make-up artist. "With the lovely bride @poppydelevingne yesterday," wrote Charlotte. "Congratulations to Poppy and James! #makeupbycharlottetilbury".

        View post on Twitter

      Poppy tied the knot with James Cook at St Paul's church in Knightsbridge on Friday afternoon.The bride was accompanied by her father, property developer Charles, who held her hand as he escorted her to the car from the hotel she had been getting ready in.Poppy was flanked by her younger sister Cara, 21, who wore a cap-sleeved V-neck white Chanel bridesmaid gown, and her elder sister Chloe, 29.

      From left to right: Chloe Delevingne, Poppy Delevingne, Charlotte Tilbury and Cara Delevingne

      Chloe, who is heavily pregnant, opted for a lilac Victoria Beckham dress which the former Spice Girl wore in September 2013.Shortly before the ceremony, Charles Delevingne confirmed that his daughter would have the 17 bridesmaids she was rumoured to have asked to be in her bridal party. "Yes, that is true," he said as he prepared to take Poppy to the church. "I'm very excited."
