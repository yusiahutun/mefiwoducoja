    Robinne Lee Career

    Robinne Lee started her acting career as a part of the ensemble cast of the romantic comedy Hav Palebtu in 1997. In 2000 she played in the television film The Runaway alongside Debbi Morgan. Lee also guest-starred on American supernatural drama television series Buffy the Vampire Slayer.
