    Adele issues 'warning' to concertgoers after fans 'forget show etiquette'

    09 Oct 2024, 00:00

    Adele has issued a ‘warning’ to concertgoers at her latest Vegas show as she says “people are forgetting show etiquette.” No need to worry though, as in true Adele style, her warning was as hilarious as her. The star joked about what she would do to fans if they ‘dared’ to throw something at her on stage, which seems to be on the rise lately.

    The Someone Like You singer, Adele, is currently on her Las Vegas residency ‘Weekends With Adele’ which is running from June through to November. With five consecutive months on stage, the music star is giving her fans warnings early on to make sure nothing unwanted is thrown toward her.

    Adele ‘warns’ concertgoers not to throw items on stage

    Adele stunned in a floor-length black gown while taking to the stage at her recent Vegas gig, but the star didn’t come to play as she issued a ‘warning’ to fans whilst holding a T-shirt cannon.

    Speaking to the crowd, The Rolling In The Deep singer said: “Have you noticed how people are forgetting f****** show etiquette at the moment, people just throwing s*** on stage, have you seen them?

    I f****** dare you. Dare you throw something at me and I’ll f****** kill you,” she joked towards the crowd. She then shot a T-Shirt from her cannon into the audience while exclaiming: “Stop throwing things at the artist when you can shoot it to people,” before erupting into a fit of laughter.

    Bebe Rexha has cell phone thrown at her on stage

    Unfortunately, a few artists have had things thrown at them on stage lately, including Harry Styles, who had a skittle thrown at his eye. However, the biggest incident of most recent weeks was when Bebe Rexha had a cell phone thrown at her on stage.

    The incident happened at her New York show a couple of weeks ago. The singer collapsed to the floor and was later taken to hospital for health treatment, where she received stitches above her left eye.

    The star took to her Instagram after the incident to share pictures of her bruised eye with stitches, captioning the post: “I’m good.”

    She appeared to be smiling with her thumbs up as a number of fans and celebs took to the post to share their well wishes.

    Demi Lovato wrote: “Love you girly.. I’m so sorry this happened to you.”

    “Love you mami,” wrote TV American Idol judge Katy Perry.

    Fans say Adele is ‘so real’ after video goes viral

    Of course, Adele fans united on social media to laugh about yet another iconic moment from the worldwide superstar. Recently, she had Bravoverse in a ‘chokehold’ as she mentioned the infamous Vanderpump Rules ‘Scandoval’ at one of her shows.

    “Adele is so real for this,” wrote one fan.

    “I would PAY to see Adele roundhouse someone,” joked another.

    Another hilarious fan wrote: “Let them know you’re from Tottenham Adele!”

    “She is so real and genuine,” penned another.

    We’re sure no one’s going to be throwing anything at her on stage in a hurry!
