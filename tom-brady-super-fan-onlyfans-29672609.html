    Stunning model Veronika Rajek has once again left the jaws of her fans wide open after going 'full Barbie' in a tiny pink bikini.

    The OnlyFans star posed in the dazzling two-piece and posted a selection of photos on Instagram where the Slovakian has over 4.6million followers.

    She captioned the post: "Barbie mood" following the recent announcement that the movie "Barbie" starring Margot Robbie will be released in July this year.

    One fan commented: "Looking great, better than Barbie".

    The model completed the pink aesthetic with matching earrings and lipstick as she was sunning herself in a luxury villa in Cancun, Mexico.

    When she's not in Mexico, or her native Slovakia, Rajek can be seen at Raymond James Stadium in Tampa, Florida to watch NFL icon Tom Brady.

    While the seven-time Super Bowl champion is now retired, having announced the news in February, watching the quarterback in action is something she can tick off her bucket list.

    Having visited America's sunny state last December, the stunner posted a pic of herself wearing a TB12 jersey as she paid tribute to the former three-time NFL MVP.

    She captioned the post: "I saw the legend, and if somebody asks me again if I love Brady, yes I love him, and show me somebody who doesn't.

    "Even his haters love him because they know he is the [GOAT], thank you for an amazing show."

    Amid rumours that Rajek and Brady are dating, she spoke to MARCA about what she looks for in a man and specifies what she is not impressed by.

    "I am looking for men to be honest and to be funny. You don't impressive me with cars, I don't care if you have a BMW or a Mercedes... it just needs four wheels and that's it. I'm not bothered by expensive clothes either," she admitted.

    She added: "I prefer an athletic man, it's important that you do sports three or four times a week. If you don't have this similarity with me, then that's not good.

    "I don't care about hair or eye colour. The most important is having that connection, that click with someone. Of course, ultimately women are looking for a man who will be a good partner and father."
