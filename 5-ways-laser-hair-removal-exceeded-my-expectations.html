    5 Ways Laser Hair Removal Exceeded My Expectations

    Makeup artist and content creator Steph Mathews is known among her social media followers for DIY beauty hacks, drugstore hauls, and low-effort yet high-glam routine videos. For the mom of three, saving time and money—without skimping on self-care—is a priority. That was a key factor in her decision to finally try laser hair removal.

    "Before getting it, I shaved from head to toe daily," says Mathews. "It felt like a job. Not having to worry about body hair anymore is so huge." To keep the treatment convenient and cost-effective, she chose Milan Laser Hair Removal, which has over 300 locations across the U.S. and payment plans to fit anyone's monthly budget.

    Here, Mathews shares what she learned from her experience at Milan Laser Hair Removal and why she's never going back to shaving.

    1. The Results Are Impressive

    Milan Laser Hair Removal uses an innovative, medical-grade laser that treats all skin tones safely and effectively with minimal pain or risk. "On average, 7 to 10 sessions result in over 95 percent reduction of hair on the treated area," says Colleen Papek, president of Milan Laser Hair Removal.

    Mathews first started her treatments, each scheduled five weeks apart, at Milan Laser in February 2022, and after just five sessions, she saw a major difference. She took a break over the summer to enjoy the sun without worrying about her skin and then resumed her sessions in the winter; skin is more sensitive to UV rays immediately after laser hair removal.

    2. The Sessions Are a Breeze

    Mathews was pleasantly surprised by how quick and painless each of her Milan Laser sessions was. "I was expecting to be there for a 45-minute appointment because that's what I was used to for a wax," she says. "But I was in and out the door on a lunch break for my legs, underarms, and a Brazilian."

    As for how the treatment feels, Mathews describes it as "a walk in the park"—especially compared to the repeated yanks of waxing.

    Milan Laser is committed to providing a great customer experience. "Our teams make it very easy for clients and create a welcoming environment for everyone," says Papek.

    Courtesy of Steph Mathews

    3. There's No Between-Appointments Stubble

    With removal methods like waxing and sugaring, you’re required to regrow hair between treatments. "I used to be a waxer," Mathews says. "First of all, it hurts, and second, that in-between time when I had to just live with stubble really bothered me." In contrast, laser removal is performed on shaved skin, so there's no need for stubble between appointments. Each time you go back, you'll notice that your hair becomes more sparse, requiring less shaving. "Even after one treatment, clients can see a 10 to 20 percent reduction," says Papek.

    4. It Boosts Confidence

    Mathews feels the self-assurance she's gained is truly priceless. "When I first started dating my husband, I would shave in the morning, and then if we had a date that night, I would have to run home and shave again," she says. "I was so nervous, like, 'Oh, he's going to touch my leg,' because if I didn't shave right before, I would have stubble—like a five-o'clock shadow."

    She adds that she used to be the "sarong queen" at the beach; she felt the need to cover up because she was self-conscious about putting her razor burn and ingrown hairs on display, two things laser hair removal also helps get rid of. "It stinks to not feel confident in your own skin," she says. "Laser hair removal has given me the security to wear whatever I want." Proof of this new care-free, hair-free chapter: Mathews didn't need to pack a razor on her recent honeymoon.

    Courtesy of Milan Laser Hair Removal

    5. The Lifetime Guarantee Provides Peace of Mind

    Mathews looked into several laser hair removal companies before landing on Milan Laser. Trust was one of the biggest factors in her decision. According to Papek, the company performs more than 60,000 treatments a month. "We only do laser hair removal; this allows us to focus on doing it better than anybody else," she says. "That’s why we are the experts."

    Mathews also appreciated how transparent Milan Laser is. One of her first questions at the initial consultation was: "What happens if my hair grows back?" While laser hair removal is generally a permanent solution, it can require maintenance. With the Milan Laser Unlimited Package™, you pay one price and get as many sessions as you need to maintain smooth, lasting results. "We want everybody to be able to get laser hair removal and never have to worry about paying more down the road, that is the Milan Laser guarantee." says Papek.
