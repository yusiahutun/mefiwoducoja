    Customisable, refillable and offering joyous escapism: meet Carolina Herrera Makeup, the first ever cosmetics line from the vivacious fashion house.

    This lands as many high fashion brands branch into beauty; from Gucci to Hermès and Jimmy Choo. But Carolina Herrera's point of difference is making make-up an accessory. Think add-ons of punchy printed cases, exuberant tassels and charms. 'Ready-to-wear collectibles', if you will.

    Courtesy

    "Traditionally, make-up is something that you keep out of sight whether on your bathroom shelf or in your vanity pouch. But this is an entirely new and disruptive way of approaching beauty," says Carolina A. Herrera, beauty creative director at Carolina Herrera Makeup (and daughter to the designer, Carolina Herrera). "We wanted to give women an opportunity to flaunt their make-up, unapologetically, like a piece of fabulous jewellery."

    As much as "disrupting" the sector, it brings some joy in testing times. The rainbow hues and playful componentry grants welcome escapism whether you're venturing out or not. Plus, of course, it's a highly Instagrammable concept.

    Courtesy

    "Make-up is the finishing touch that elevates an outfit, but it’s also a statement within itself," adds Wes Gordon, Carolina Herrera creative director. "We wanted each piece to double up as an eye-catching, high-fashion accessory that you could literally wear and show off. It’s all about joy of living and that ‘fantasticness’ that’s so appealing in fashion today."

    Related Story

    The line launches with refillable lipsticks in a palette of 36 shades (16 matte, 12 satin and eight sheer); refillable powders in eight shades; a highlighter and mattifier; plus Carolina Herrera Makeup brushes. Accessories include ten printed cases, ten tassels and three charms compatible with the lipsticks; four printed cases for the compacts; and a Carolina Herrera-engraved jewellery-like ring that clips onto all.

    Shop the range exclusively at Harrods now. Prices range from £8-£29.
