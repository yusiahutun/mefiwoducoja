    Sharing reels is the new way of growing on Instagram. Do you need some help keeping up with the trends to enlarge your following? We got you covered!

    Popular trends to try are mostly the transitions as expected. Transitions create an excitement in the viewer, so make sure to use them. 

    Top Instagram Trends in 2022

    The Two Finger Transition 

    Use your index and middle finger to go up and down the camera, and change into your best self from your rather harsh looking self.

    Are You New Here?

    Well, isn’t it nice of us to make fun people? Use this sound to share something your friend does differently, or simply mock them for being different. 

    Abracadabra 

    Instagram creators show us all how it’s done! Whether it’s fashion, beauty, travelling or cooking. Have you ever had a question about how you do something and how someone else can’t even imagine doing? Well it’s time to answer that question.

    Is It All Real?

    Do you have a video of a memory that just feels like a dream? (I hope you do!). Then use this Harry Potter sound to share your memory, in the most wholesome way possible.

    Trending Songs for Reels

    Songs are also as important as doing the trends on Reels. Here is our selection of trending songs that might help you with your reach:

    • Infinity by James Young
    • Sacrifice by The Weeknd
    • Balenciaga by T3NZU
    • Frozen by Madonna, Sickick
    • Love Grows by Edison Lighthouse

    These were our suggestions for you to kill your Instagram Reels game! Keep in mind though, that these trends change frequently, and the sooner you catch them the better! 

    Have we missed a trend? Which trends & songs have you seen on your Reels tab lately? Share with us in the comments below?
