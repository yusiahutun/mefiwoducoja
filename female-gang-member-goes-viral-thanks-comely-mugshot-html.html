    Female gang member goes viral thanks to her comely mugshot

    A California gang member is being dubbed the newhot felon thanks to her comely mugshot going viral. Mirella Ponce was arrested on two felony charges after she was stopped by police officers in a vehicle containing an infant child and a loaded gun in Fresno on Monday.

    18 Jul 2024, 00:00

    A California gang member is being dubbed the new hot felon thanks to her comely mugshot going viral. 

    Mirella Ponce was arrested on two felony charges after she was stopped by police officers in a vehicle containing an infant child and a loaded gun in Fresno on Monday.

    The 20-year-old, who is a member of the Tiny Rascal Gang (TRG), was busted along with two other members inside the vehicle, police say. 

    She's been charged with carrying a concealed firearm and carrying a stolen firearm - both are felonies. Her bail has been set at $50,000 for each charge.  

    Information about her arrest, her mugshot photo, and a photo of the lilac colored handgun she allegedly had in her possession during the stop were shared to the Fresno Police Department's Facebook page. 

    Hot felon? Mirella Ponce, a California gang member, is being dubbed the new hot felon thanks to her comely mugshot (above) going viral. The 20-year-old, who is a member of the Tiny Rascal Gang, has several tattoos including one that reads 'Love is Pain' across her chest

    She was arrested on a weapon charge after she was stopped by police officers in a vehicle containing her and her infant child in Fresno on Monday. Authorities retrieved the lilac colored gun and bullets pictured above from the car

    The 20-year-old is being held on two felony charges in a Fresno jail. Above is her booking information

    Ponce's numerous chest and neck tattoos are visible in the arrest photo where she can be seen gazing into the camera. 

    Since being shared on Wednesday, the police department's post with her mugshot has since gone viral with nearly 4,000 shares and thousands of comments. 

    Some of the Facebook users have even offered to bail Ponce out of jail. 

    'Ima (sic) just go bail her out. Free this hot gangster chick,' one Facebook user commented on the post.

    Since Fresno Police Department shared her mugshot to their Facebook page on Wednesday, her photo has gone viral with many commenting on her good looks and make-up

    One man also wrote: 'How much the bail is I'll pay it'. 

    One of the most popular comments reads: 'The only crime she committed was stealing my heart.'  

    'What a shame though, she could of been a stripper or something with those good looks,' another user wrote.

    Some users noted how she could have been a stripper while another person said they hope she 'turns her life around for her children'

    Another user noted how great Ponce's make-up looked in her booking photo. 

    'She's beautiful tho that highlight poppin (sic),' one woman wrote on the post.

    Another person encouraged her to turn her life around for her children. 

    'Didnt (sic) know TRG are still around.. Beautiful lady.. Hope she works her beauty and puts it to work..Hope she changes her life around for her and her children,' one user wrote. 

    TRG is a California based gang that is predominantly Cambodian-American. It started in the 1980s and it's estimated that there are 10,000 members across the country. 

    Ponce's mugshot is not the first to go viral. In 2014, Jeremy Meeks was arrested on firearm charges and grand theft.

    His subsequent mugshot went viral on social media and earned him the moniker 'Hot Felon', which launched a modeling career for him when he was released from prison in September 2016.

    Meeks is currently dating Chloe Green, daughter of billionaire Sir Philip Green, and heiress to the Topshop empire.
