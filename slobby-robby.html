    What is Slobby Robby’s net worth?

    Introduction

    Slobby Robby (real name – Robert Hall) is an American store owner and reality TV star who sells and collects vintage clothing and memorabilia from the 1980s and 1990s, including bootleg clothing, games, toys, sneakers, jerseys, jewelry, and luxury leather bags.

    In addition, Robby refurbishes vintage pieces to make them more current.

    Biography

    There is not much information about his date of birth except that he is a Tucson native.

    His obsession with toys started in high school. Slobby explained in an interview: ”I think I first started obsessing over vintage my freshman year of high school.”

    In 2013, Robby opened his first pop-culture store, ”Generation Cool,” on 4th Avenue in Tucson, Arizona.

    The boutique is specialized in the ’80s and ’90s apparel and pop culture memorabilia.

    ”We only buy the ’80s and ’90s vintage pieces,” said Hall. He added: ”Top shelf, iconic stuff, bright, colorful stuff that suits our style, suits my style.”

    In 2018, the Slobbys World television series was released. It also ran on Netflix. The show follows Hall and his staff as they search for and evaluate unique collectibles, toys, and clothing from across the US.

    ”This stuff is my life,” Robby said in the first episode of the reality TV show. He continued: ”My life revolves around the ’80s and ’90s periods and capturing the nostalgia and the feeling I had when I was a kid from those eras.”

    In 2019, Hall opened a new location at 3540 W Sahara Ave, Las Vegas.
