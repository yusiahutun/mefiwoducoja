    Barry Keoghan and Sabrina Carpenter were one of the cutest couples at the Vanity Fair Oscars Party last night, especially with their poses in front of photographers.

    Sabrina Carpenter just enjoyed a once-in-a-lifetime experience opening for Taylor Swift on her Eras Tour, both in Australia and Singapore. But the celebrity had time to make it back in time for the Oscars, and enjoyed the after party with her actor beau. We think Barry Keoghan and Sabrina Carpenter are the new couple on the block – move over Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce.

    They were spotted before Christmas enjoying dinner in Brentwood which first sparked romance rumors.

    It comes after he broke up with ex-girlfriend Alyson Kierans in July, which he shares a son with, Brando.
