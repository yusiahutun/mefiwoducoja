    Christina Aguilera has fans ‘freaking out’ once again as she shows off her A+ fashion sense and this time she’s showing off her stunning body in a see-through corset.

    The music star has been on the scene since 1999, when she broke through with her iconic hit, Genie In A Bottle. Almost two and a half decades later, the icon is still going strong, announcing her “intimate, seductive and sophisticated new show” in Las Vegas. And what an announcement post it was.

    Christina Aguilera sends fans wild in see-through corset

    Taking to her 9 million Instagram followers, the Candyman singer had fans going wild for two things. One, the excitement of her upcoming intimate Vegas show, and secondly her stunning promo pic for the residency.

    In the snap, the 42-year-old can be seen wearing a plunging black corset, with a mesh design on her midriff, whilst seductively posting, showing off her long, toned legs.

    The star also took to her stories to share some behind-the-scenes footage from the promo photoshoot, sharing a video montage of outtakes, where she can be seen in different poses showing off her body, and prominent smoky eye look.

    Her fashion sense isn’t the only eye-catching thing in the new post. The singer also debuted her short blonde bob in the promo.

    Fans definitely approved of the new look as one penned: “Short hair is fire on you!”

    “You got me freaking out over this [fire emoji]” said another.

    “It’s giving burlesque YES,” exclaimed another.

    The star teases new music

    Along with her residency, it looks like the pop star is also set to release some new music. Talk about spoiling fans!

    Taking to her stories that same day, XTina shared a tweet from X, formerly known as Twitter from Big Latto, which read: “Should we tell them?”

    The star then responded to him in a quote tweet by saying: “I think it’s time babe…”

    Well, whatever it is, we’re definitely ready!
