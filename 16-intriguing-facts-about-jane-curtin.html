    A Pioneering Saturday Night Live Star

    Jane Curtin gained fame as one of the original cast members of the legendary sketch comedy show, “Saturday Night Live.” Her razor-sharp wit and comedic timing made her a standout performer, and she became the first female anchor on the show’s popular “Weekend Update” segment.
