    25 hairstyles for over 50 and overweight that are so cute

    Finding the near-perfect hairstyles for over 50 and overweight women can be challenging. But this doesn't mean they don't exist. Several incredibly youthful hairstyles for over 50 will make you feel more beautiful and energetic.

    As humans grow older, weight, skin, and hair change. The hair often becomes thinner and, in some cases, more brittle. Regardless, you can still rock the trendiest hairstyles. There are plenty of options, and if you choose the right hairstyle, you can look as cool as you were in your 20s.

    Best hairstyles for women over 50

    Several hairstyles will help you age beautifully, but according to Hairstyles Feed, these are the 25 best.

    1. Blonde bob

    The classic blonde bob hairstyle has been around for quite a long time and is loved by women in their 50s because of its mature appeal. It is so eye-catching and elegant at first sight.

    2. Short blonde hair

    This lovely short blonde hair is excellent for heavy-set women with round faces. It's undoubtedly one of the most flattering hairstyles for those over 50 and overweight.

    The short blonde hairstyle includes a side part and face-framing layers, making a face appear less rounded.

    3. Medium gray

    The medium gray is one of the best medium-length hairstyles for over 50 and overweight. Some older women cover their gray hair with beautiful colors, but others love it natural.

    The original hair can look lovely when styled with the right length and shape. Keeping it medium makes it look and feel elegant - that's why most women in their 50s are particular fans of this length.

    4. Mermaid bob

    Trying out different fun hair colors is not only limited to the young. You can also do it in your 50s to identify a color that makes you look cute. The best colors to try are purple and pink because they give your hair a mermaid vibe that's cool and subtle.

    5. Gray bob with dark roots

    The typical bob hairstyle is casual, dainty, and low-maintenance. You can add dark roots to make the silver appear young and well-groomed.

    6. Long Balayage

    Your long hair can be styled in various ways, from wavy to straight. Or you can create layers or make it shaggy for the hair to look voluminous. But if neither of these interests you, then you should try the long Balayage. Today, Balayage is among the trendiest hairstyles for overweight women as it looks so gorgeous.

    7. Light blonde

    The light blonde hairstyle will make you look chic even in your 50s. The best part is that you can choose from various shades, such as vanilla, platinum, ash, silver, etc.

    8. Pink pixie cut

    Colorful hair looks great in most over 50 and overweight women with round faces. The pink pixie cut is an attractive hairstyle that makes you look straight from a fairy tale. It's great for people with naturally light hair, but you can also bleach it if you want to try this hairstyle.

    9. Short spike

    The short spiky hairstyle is one of the baddie styles that women can rock in their 50s. It is a simple design; the blonde hair is cut short, and spikes are created in the middle giving cool vibes. You can further spice your look with accessories such as earrings.

    10. Bob, with a side bang

    The curtain and side bang styles have been around for years, which makes them ideal for women over 50. This style will perfectly cover your forehead and give thinner hair a fuller appearance.

    11. Short undercut pixie

    This short haircut is visually slimming, thus, best for overweight women. It's one of the best hairstyles for fat faces and double chins. The style works well on both straight and stubborn curly hair.

    12. Feminine feathered hairstyle

    This medium-length hairstyle with feathered flipped-up layers is the best slimming hair hack for overweight's. The style does a lot to narrow a broad face and make it appear less chubby.

    13. Ombre hairstyle

    The Ombre hair coloring technique makes your hair appear super gorgeous. This hairstyle will make your face instantly attractive, and the best part is the color doesn't touch the hair roots. So it's a safe way to add color to your hair without damaging it. Plus, it is a low-maintenance hairstyle, meaning you won't spend much.

    14. Pink Balayage

    If you're considering upgrading your hairstyle, go for the pink Balayage. The base hair is brown, and the top is pink, making it look elegant. If you're going for a date, wedding, or vacation, you should make your hair curly.

    15. Plus-size wavy hairstyle

    This plus-size wavy hairstyle is one of the youthful hairstyles for those over 50. The curly ends make this style great and keep your looks classy and adorable. If you'd love to keep the chubby parts of your face in the shadows, you should do this hairstyle. It distracts attention from those parts.

    It's among the short hairstyles for plus size women over 50. This refreshing short haircut allows you to embrace your natural colors. The salt-and-pepper short-shag hairstyle is easy to maintain, minimalistic, and classy. This hairstyle has different variations, so you'll have options.

    17. Bun updo hairstyle

    The bun is among the most practical updos. However, most plus-sized women avoid it because it makes their faces appear chubby. But this is not true. There are various bun hairstyles, so pick one that matches your facial structure.

    18. Curly lob hairstyle

    All women love luscious-looking curly hair. The curly lob is among the best hairstyles for fat and double chins. It perfectly complements the face and makes one look younger. You can maintain the curls using a suitable hair care routine.

    19. Medium wavy hair

    This hairstyle is a classic look and a game-changer. This is a great hairstyle choice if you don't want to strain your hair a lot. The wavy design makes your hair appear smooth and beautiful. Ensure you moisturize your hair ends to make them shiny.

    20. White blonde shaggy pixie haircut

    This hairstyle for women over 50 varies in colors and shapes. The short pixie haircut is a never-dying classic. They tend to be sassy and playful but so age-appropriate and mature. You can add a touch to the spiky layer to make it chic.

    21. Sleek pony hairstyle

    The sleek pony hairstyle is versatile and suitable for various settings. The pony will create a statement because it is in vogue now. You can upgrade your sleek pony by wrapping its rubber band part with a section of your hair.

    22. Space buns

    It's among the few hairstyles that look good on a chubby face. Space buns are among the most practical and stylish hairstyles of the day. These buns are created on the sides of the head, and they help balance the weight's space. This makes it a great hairstyle choice for plus-sized girls.

    Are you looking for the right hairstyle that'll have you glowing even in your 50s? This hairstyle stands out and is best for brunettes with thicker short hair. It's a fashionable way to cover your face's side and works well for a full face and double chin.

    24. Compact shag

    This is one of the classic hairstyles that is making a comeback. Billie Eilish is among the celebrities that rock this hairstyle. When discussing a shag haircut, some people picture a messy look with layers everywhere. But most people do keep their shag hair neat and compact.

    25. Teased back

    The teased-back hairstyle is a popular hairstyle choice among women in their 50s. It gives thin hair a voluminous look and also complements some facial structures. You can wear this hairstyle regularly or just on occasion.
