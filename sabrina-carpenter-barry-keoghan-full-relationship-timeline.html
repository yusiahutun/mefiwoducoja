    Sabrina Carpenter and Barry Keoghan's Full Relationship Timeline

    Its real. Its happening. Sabrina Carpenter and Barry Keoghan are dating. The Feather singer and the Saltburn actor were first linked in December 2023, after Carpenters breakup with singer Shawn Mendes and Keoghans split from girlfriend Alyson Sandro, with whom he shares a baby son, Brando.

    06 Feb 2024, 00:00

    It’s real. It’s happening. Sabrina Carpenter and Barry Keoghan are dating.

    The “Feather” singer and the Saltburn actor were first linked in December 2023, after Carpenter’s breakup with singer Shawn Mendes and Keoghan’s split from girlfriend Alyson Sandro, with whom he shares a baby son, Brando.

    Since then, the two stars have gone public with their romance—sending us all into a spiral.

    See everything we know, below.

    April 13, 2024: They enjoy a double date with Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce at Coachella.

    Keoghan and Carpenter’s Coachella date overflows with star power when they meet up with Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce. The two celebrity couples look so in love and stylish while they wait for a music act to start.

    “Nonsense” singer Carpenter looks effortlessly beautiful in an oversize black leather jacket, black-framed glasses, a small beige purse, and diamond stud earrings. Dunkirk actor Keoghan, meanwhile, shows off his desert style in a white Versace tank top, beige plaid shorts, a matching handkerchief tied around his neck, and a cobalt-blue baseball cap.

    April 12, 2024: Barry supports Sabrina at Coachella.

    Keoghan looks like a man in love—and a bit like Regina's mom from Mean Girls—while watching Carpenter perform at Coachella on day one. In videos posted to X, the actor films the “Feather” singer’s set and cheers—not taking his eyes off of her for even a second.

    In another video, the two are seen riding in a golf cart together backstage.

    March 16, 2024: Barry takes a pic with Travis Kelce.

    It’s a best practice to befriend the members of your significant other’s inner circle, right? Keoghan certainly seems to think so. He links up with Kansas City Chiefs tight end—and Taylor Swift beau—Travis Kelce at a Justin Timberlake concert in Los Angeles. The two snap a photo together, which Keoghan posts to his Instagram story with the caption: “When BK met TK 🙏🏻 🙏🏻.”

    courtesy of Barry Keoghan via Instagram

    The Saltburn actor looks cool in a matching track jacket and shorts set in a beige, white, and brown pixel pattern, along with a black baseball cap, red strap watch, and crisp white sneakers and ankle socks. The football star looks suave in baggy reflective silver pants, a black collared long-sleeved shirt with small blue decorative lines all over, white sneakers, and a thick silver chain necklace.

    March 10, 2024: Sabrina and Barry go to the Oscars after-party together.

    The stars hard-launch their relationship at Vanity Fair’s annual Oscars after-party. Though they did not take pictures on the red carpet together earlier, they are seen posing for a photograph inside the venue. Carpenter wears a sheer black Tory Burch dress, while Keoghan sports a tweed collared jacket over a white tank and black pants; he also accessorizes with a very meaningful accessory: a Taylor Swift Eras Tour friendship bracelet with a heart charm and Carpenter’s name on it! (As readers know, Carpenter has been opening for Swift on her worldwide tour.)

    Dave Benett/VF24//Getty Images

    Taylor Hill//Getty Images

    Later in the night, the couple is seen heading home, and Carpenter wears Keoghan’s jacket over her dress, while he walks out in only his tank and pants.

    BACKGRID

    March 3, 2024: Barry supports Sabrina at her performance in Singapore.

    Keoghan is in the audience, looking as smitten as ever, as Carpenter takes the stage as the opener for Taylor Swift when the Eras Tour comes to Singapore.

    Throughout the performance, the actor—wearing a red bomber jacket and matching baseball cap—can’t stop smiling and gazing at his girlfriend with adoring eyes. The cuteness peaks when she sings her outro for “Nonsense.” It goes: “He said that he wishes he was on me / Got me wetter than the Jewel Changi / Singapore, I hope you like my song-y.”

    For most of the song, Keoghan hides his smile behind the show badge hanging around his neck, but as Carpenter wraps up, he happily cheers with his arms up.

    “Oh, that man love LOVES her,” one fan comments on TikTok. “HE’S SO IN LOVE,” writes another. “Stop I love them so much,” posts a third.

    February 12, 2024: They go on a hot date.

    The couple is spotted on a dinner date at Los Angeles celebrity hot spot Delilah. Later in the night, they are seen heading to cocktail lounge Ysabel to continue their night.

    Carpenter looks fabulous in a black sleeveless mock-neck minidress from Reformation, which she pairs with a black leather jacket, black knee-high Reformation boots, and a matching leather shoulder bag. Her blonde hair is down, straight, and she wears natural makeup.

    Keoghan goes cool and casual in a classic white T-shirt, tan trousers, a bright red bomber jacket, and white sneakers. He accessorizes with a single silver chain necklace.

    BACKGRID

    February 10 and 11, 2024: Barry is seen at Sabrina’s house.

    On Saturday, the pair are seen leaving Los Angeles’s Hotel Bel-Air and heading to their car. And on Sunday morning, Keoghan is spotted heading out of Carpenter’s house in the Hollywood Hills, wearing a black tracksuit and gray sneakers.

    February 4, 2024: They make their first public appearance as a couple.

    Keoghan and Carpenter attend their first public event as a couple, stepping out together for W Magazine’s star-studded Grammys after-party. Though Carpenter walks the red carpet at the music awards ceremony solo, she is seen partying with the actor later in the evening, at L.A.’s Bar Marmont.

    Carpenter wears a sheer white floral top and matching skirt, while Keoghan opts for a light blue ribbed sweater and dark blue pants.

    January 13, 2024: They go on an artsy date.

    The singer and the actor are spotted on a sweet date at an art gallery in Los Angeles, in coordinating black looks.

    December 2, 2023: They’re seen together for the first time.

    Carpenter and Keoghan are photographed on a night out together for the first time, in L.A.’s Brentwood neighborhood. The pop singer looks sleek in a black leather trench coat, gray top, wide-leg blue jeans, and chunky black leather boots. She accessorizes with a white handbag and a black hair bow. Keoghan is cool in a white collared sweater and black pants.

    September 28, 2023: They meet.

    The pair reportedly first meet when they both attend the Givenchy Spring/Summer 2024 runway show during Paris Fashion Week, though they are not photographed together.
