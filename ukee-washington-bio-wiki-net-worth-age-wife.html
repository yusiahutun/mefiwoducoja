    Ukee Washington Bio & Wiki

    Ukee Washington was born on 20th August 1958, in Philadelphia, PA. His father Ulysses S. Washington was an American college football player and coach at Delaware State University whereas the details of his mother are unknown. He is the godfather of Philadelphia Eagles safety Nate Allen.

    As per his nationality, Ukee is American and belongs to African-American ethnic background. Washington is the second cousin of actor Denzel Washington. After completing his high school, Ukee went to the University of Richmond for higher studies.
