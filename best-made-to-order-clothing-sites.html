    The 8 Best Sites for Made-to-Order Clothing and Accessories

    04 Oct 2024, 00:00

    If you’re ready for a wardrobe that fits perfectly, then the sites that offer bespoke, make-to-order garments should be on your radar. In contrast to fast fashion, this method of manufacturing clothes also creates much less waste.

    With a few clicks, you can create perfectly tailored shirts, pants, dresses, suits, and more. Here are eight of the best sites you can use.

    1. Eshakti

    With its vast selection of clothing for women, Eshakti is a fantastic resource for made-to-order clothing. Whether you want clothes for work, vacation, a wedding, or casual everyday wear, it has a bit of everything, with a strong focus on dresses and tops in a variety of fabric choices.

    You can customize sleeve length, hem length, and necklines with the site’s cool eShakti FX tool, which lets you view all the garment changes in real-time. As a bonus, many of the dresses and skirts include pockets.

    With a size range from XS to 6X, the site's made-to-measure clothes cost around $50 to $100. If you're looking for quality alternatives to Shein clothes and similar fast fashion styles, then Eshakti is well worth a look.

    2. Tailored Jeans

    There's nothing like a pair of jeans that fit perfectly, and the Tailored Jeans store offers a dizzying array of options. Bespoke suits are great, sure, but completely custom-built jeans will probably get a lot more use.

    There are plenty of online options for custom jeans, but few sites match the selection offered by Tailored Jeans. Made for all sizes and shapes, the site also offers many different styles, colors, and fabrics.

    Start by choosing your fabric, rise, and fit type. Next, get into the details by choosing the type of stitching for your back pocket, and even the types of belt loops you prefer.

    By following the site's series of dropdown menus, designing your own pair of jeans is simple and fun. Prices range from around $70 to $100.

    3. Hockerty

    Dress sharp with a suit made to your exact measurements. Hockerty, and its sister site for women's suiting Sumissura, have everything you need.

    You can choose your fabric, then personalize details about your lapels, pockets, and more. Adjust styles in real-time with the online 3D suit visualizer to check out different fabric and color choices easily.

    In addition to suits, Hockerty also sells custom shirts, pants, blazers, and outerwear. Custom suits start at around $300, all of them made to fit each customer—the site does not even list standard sizes.

    4. Proper Cloth

    When it comes to fashion staples, the classic button-down shirt is one of the most versatile pieces around. Proper Cloth offers a gigantic variety of fabric choices and many size options.

    When it comes to size selection, you can answer a quick questionairre, choose a standard size, measure yourself, measure a well-fitting shirt, or even mail the Proper Cloth people a shirt to measure.

    In addition, the first shirt comes with complementary refits. Aside from working with an in-person tailor, it's one of the most effective ways to get a shirt that fits perfectly.

    With over 30 options for collars, a dozen cuffs, and nine pocket styles, the site is all about customization. Starting at $95, these shirts let you adjust every last detail.

    5. Alamour

    If you have an event planned, then the amazing gowns at Alamour are well worth a look. Customize the neckline, color, and length to get the look you want with many of these dresses.

    Search for Made to Order options to check out the customizable gowns. Sizes range from XXS to 9XL, and you can email customer care to double-check these options.

    There's also a whole section for bridal looks in particular. Details like sheer lace, structured bodices, long trains, and more make it easy to create your custom-made dream dress.

    Priced from about $200 to $600, the gowns do require some time to construct, so buyers are encouraged to place orders at least four months before an event. As long as you aren't pressed for time, however, these custom-made formal dresses are perfect for proms, weddings, cocktail parties, and travel.

    6. Frilly

    You can get customized, fashion-forward pants, jumpsuits, dresses, and much more at Frilly. If you love browsing luxury fashion websites for inspiration, then these customizable options offer similar looks at lower price points.

    Pick out a skirt, jumpsuit, jacket, or anything else that catches your attention. Next, tweak the hemline, sleeves, neckline, and more to your liking.

    At prices from around $90 to $400, Frilly offers a little bit of everything. Sizes range from XXS to L.

    7. Adelante

    Create sandals, boots, loafers, and more at Adelante. You can buy shoes as shown, or choose to experiment with different colors.

    See what each pair looks like with some slight tweaks to the styling, or go all-out with a different color for every portion of the shoe. You can even select different sizes and widths for the left and right shoes.

    With so many options for both men's and women's shoes, Adelante gives you the chance to become a footwear designer with a few clicks. Prices range from about $130 to $300, and sizes run from four to 17 (men's) and four to 15 (women's).

    8. Laudi Vidni

    If you've ever wanted a custom-built bag for your specific needs, then the Laudi Vidni site should be on your radar. Buy the bags as-is, make some slight personalizations, or go for a full-custom design.

    Whether you need a work tote, an everyday bag, or an evening purse, the site offers plenty of options. Crossbody purses, shoulder bags, belt bags, clutches, and backpacks are all available, many with multiple styles.

    Choose a leather color, lining design, hardware color, strap length, and more. Your customized options pop up on the bag in real-time, so you get a sense of exactly how it will look.

    Prices start at about $145 for a wristlet to around $1,000 for a large carryall, with many different price points along the way. Laptop bags, toiletry bags, and duffle bags are available too.

    Create Made-to-Order Clothes With These Fashionable Websites

    Whether you're looking for formalwear, professional outfits, or everyday clothes, ordering made-to-order clothes helps you build a quality, tailored wardrobe. You'll get garments that fit great while avoiding a lot of the waste and poor quality often associated with fast fashion.

    When it's time to level up your wardrobe, consider clothes that are perfectly tailored for you. And if you need to organize your outfits, tech can come to the rescue again.
