    31 May 2024, 00:00

    As the world's most stylish women descended on the fashion capitals for the SS20 shows, one trend seemed to emerge surprisingly strongly from the street-style set: Matrix-inspired leather. Top-to-toe black leather ensembles, long leather trench coats and tiny sunglasses have all had their place on the front row, suggesting that The Matrix's Trinity may just be the unlikely style icon of autumn/winter 2019.

    The character from the film trilogy was known for her love of leather and, just like the film's lead Neo, is a cult style hero embedded into movie history. While her liquid leather trench coats and sharp leather co-ords have come back in and out of style plenty of times over the past 20 years, our current love of everything late '90s means that the look was bound to return soon.

    Jasin Boland/Warner Bros/Kobal/Shutterstock//Shutterstock

    What is key to note though is that while this harsher look might come and go, investing in luxurious leather goods is never a bad idea if you want to have pieces in your wardrobe that you will be able to style forever.

    Below, we round up five different ways to channel Trinity's look for autumn/winter 2019, but with pieces that you will be able to wear time and time again.

    The leather coat

    Elsa Hosk's take on the trend is proof that even with the double injection of Matrix fashion (a longline leather blazer and skinny sunglasses), it does not have to look like fancy dress. The supermodel has made it work by teaming with tailored black trousers and a simple beige top, giving the style a softer and smarter edge.
