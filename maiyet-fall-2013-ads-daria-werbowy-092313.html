    01 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Globally-minded, high fashion brand Maiyet has worked with Daria Werbowy in its ad campaigns since its initial launch. The model's understated elegance and innate cool makes for an ideal pairing with the wearable luxury brand—and Fall 2013's campaign is no different. Set on a beach in New York, the unfussy imagery is arresting without overcomplicating it. "We have traveled to some exotic places for shoots, but NY is our home, and we just opened our very first shop here on Crosby Street, so it felt right to find a location here for the Fall season," Maiyet Creative Director, Kristy Caylor explains. "We loved the idea of this strong, interesting, girl stranded on a beach at night—maybe she's alone, maybe she's with someone else...it's mysterious, sexy, and a little bit fun all at the same time."
