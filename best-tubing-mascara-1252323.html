    What Mascara is Tubing Mascara?

    Unlike traditional mascaras which coat your eyelashes with pigments, tubing mascaras wrap each lash in water-resistant, tube-like enclosures. The bold pigments and volume-building fibers thicken, separate, and dramatically lengthen lashes. It delivers maximum clump-free definition. Although the formula is easy to remove, it resists clumping. As the formula is not oil-based, tubing mascaras do not flake or smear.

    How To Choose The Best Tubing Mascara?

    To choose the best tubing mascara, follow these simple steps:

    • Formula: The first thing to keep in mind when selecting a tubing mascara is its formulation. Check whether it uses tubing technology. The package will denote that the formula uses tube or tubular technology.
    • Features: A tubing mascara doesn't smudge, smear, run, or flake like the traditional formulas. The polymers used in the formula stick to each lash and do not budge easily. It gives your lashes volume and curl and stays for long periods.
    • Brush Type: The tubing mascaras come with different types of brushes to deliver different lash looks. Volumizing formulas come with a large, thick brush, curling formulas come with a curved brush, and lengthening formulas have a skinny brush. The formulas that provide full coverage, and natural lash looks come with a tapered brush. Choosing the correct brush type is an essential part of selecting the right mascara for your desired look.
    • Ease of Removal: The beauty of tubing mascaras is its easy removal. It can be removed with just a little warm water without the need for any makeup remover. With gentle pressure and water, the tubes come off easily. 
    • Customer Reviews: Customer reviews will let you know the performance quality of the mascara. Also, you can know whether the mascara you have picked up is a true tubing mascara or not. They will also inform you about how easy or hard the removal process is.
