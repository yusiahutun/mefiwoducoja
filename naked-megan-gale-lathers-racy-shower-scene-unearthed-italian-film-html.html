    A heavily pregnant Megan Gale posed completely nude for this month's issue of Marie Claire Australia.

    But it's not the first time the stunning Australian model has been naked in front of the camera.

    Soap and suds: Megan lathers up during a show scene on the foreign film

    The footage was then published The Fix on Thursday - and it's a treat.

    The movie is called Vacanze di Natale 2000 (Christmas Vacation 2000), which Megan landed a role in after becoming the face of phone company Omnitel (now Vodafone) in Italy in the 90s.

    Tight squeeze: Unbeknown to her, the stunner was showering with two admirers

    Despite the language barrier for non-Italian speakers, it's obvious from watching just a few scenes that the film's plot surrounds two men who adore the beautiful brunette.

    In one scene a leather-pants-and-crop-top clad Megan is stopped by the two men as she attempts to ski down a mountain.

    The men put a large coat around her and a conversation ensues.

    Surprise! Megan had no idea what was lurking behind her in the Christmas vacation film

    Oops! A naked 25-year-old Megan dropped the soap while showering in the awkward scene

    In the next scene she wears a white fluffy robe over black lingerie, the beauty sitting on a lounge with a man who is also clad in a robe, and is forced to resist his advances.

    The two adoring men take things a little too far when they surprise an unsuspecting Megan as she showers.

    All rugged up: The admired young woman eventually gives in to the chase, cuddling up on a rug in front of the fire with one of the men

    As well as Vacanze di Natale, Megan also featured in Italian film Bodyguards, Guardie del corpo and Stregata Dalla Luna.

    Megan announced her pregnancy news back in November by posting a picture of her ultrasound on Instagram.

    She was 13 weeks pregnant at that point, and is due to give birth in mid-May.

    The father of the baby-to-be is Shaun Hampson, Gale's 26-year-old AFL star boyfriend - the two began dating in late 2010.
